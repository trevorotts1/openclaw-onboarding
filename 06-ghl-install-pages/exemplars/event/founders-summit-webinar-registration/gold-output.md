## Why live, not a recording

Because the moment that actually moves a plan forward is watching someone
else's real numbers get pressure-tested out loud — the objection you didn't
think to raise, the assumption you didn't know was optional. A recording
cannot do that. A live room can.

## What we rebuild, together

- **The one metric** each of the three founders is optimizing for that is
  quietly wrong for where their business actually is right now.
- **The Q3 sequence** — what moves first, what waits, and why the order
  matters more than the list itself.
- **The single change** each founder makes on screen, live, before the
  session ends — not homework, a decision made in the room.

## Who should show up

Founders with a Q3 plan already drafted who want it pressure-tested before
they commit budget and headcount to it — not founders starting from a blank
page.
