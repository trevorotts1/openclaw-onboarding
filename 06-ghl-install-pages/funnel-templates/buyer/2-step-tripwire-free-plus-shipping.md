# 2-Step Tripwire / Free-Plus-Shipping Funnel

**id:** `2-step-tripwire-free-plus-shipping` · **category:** Buyer Funnel · **group:** buyer · **length:** short-form · **library ref:** #8

**Aliases:** Tripwire Funnel · Unboxing Funnel · Two-Step Free-Plus-Shipping Funnel · Free Plus Shipping Funnel · Free + Shipping Offer

**Source books:** The Funnel Hacker's Cookbook · Marketing Secrets Blackbook · DotCom Secrets · Expert Secrets

## When to use / trigger criteria

Acquire a **new buyer at zero net cost** on cold traffic by leading with an irresistibly cheap or free front-end (physical product, sample, or low-cost info product) where the customer pays only shipping. The 2-step order form captures the lead on step 1 (abandoned-cart recovery) before billing on step 2. All OTO/downsell revenue is pure profit.

**Goals:** convert cold/paid traffic into first-time buyers cheaply · build a buyer list (not a lead list) · acquire customers at break-even via bump + OTOs · liquidate ad spend.

**Keywords:** free plus shipping · free + shipping · tripwire · low ticket front end · $1 offer · sample offer · unboxing · just pay shipping · buyer list · customer acquisition funnel.

**Signals:** client has a cheap/physical front-end or sample · goal is "get customers" / "self-liquidate ad spend" not "collect leads" · cold paid traffic · a clear backend value ladder exists.

**Route elsewhere when:** front-end is $27–$97 with margin goals → SLO Funnel · the free item is a book → Book Funnel · email-only, no payment → Lead/Squeeze Funnel · recurring is the core offer → Membership/Continuity.

---

## Page structure (4 pages)

1. **2-Step Order Form Page** — Sell the irresistible front-end and capture the buyer. *Step 1* collects name + shipping address (creates an abandoned-cart lead even if they bail); *Step 2* reveals billing + the order bump.
   - Hook headline + subheadline; product hero/unboxing image or 5-minute sales video; what's-included value stack vs. "you pay only shipping"; contact+address fields (step 1); payment fields + **order-bump checkbox with its own mini-sales copy** (step 2); scarcity/urgency; guarantee; testimonials/social proof.
2. **OTO 1 (One-Click Upsell) Page** — Higher-value complementary product as a one-click add-on (card on file) to raise AOV.
   - "Wait — your order isn't complete" headline; upsell VSL or short copy; one-click "Yes, add this" button; plain "No thanks" decline link.
3. **OTO 2 / Downsell Page** — Second upsell, or downsell of OTO 1 (payment plan / lite version).
   - One-click accept button; decline link.
4. **Confirmation / Offer-Wall Page** — Confirm order, deliver/ship, bond, and bridge to the next rung.
   - Order summary; shipping/delivery + digital bonus access; Attractive-Character message; next-step CTA into the backend offer.
