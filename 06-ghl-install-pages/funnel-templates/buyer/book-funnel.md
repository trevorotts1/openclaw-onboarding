## When to use / trigger criteria

A **free-plus-shipping physical book** offer where the prospect pays only shipping (typically **$7.95**). The book acquires a buyer-quality lead; an order bump on the payment step and **two OTOs** after purchase cover ad costs and generate profit. The confirmation page delivers high-value **bonus training** that bridges to a higher-priced backend offer. Use when the client has (or can produce) a book and wants a premium buyer list while liquidating ad spend.

**Goals:** acquire buyer-quality leads via a free book · establish the author as the expert / Attractive Character · cover ad costs with the bump + 2 OTOs · bridge new buyers into a higher-priced backend offer.

**Keywords:** free book · free plus shipping book · just pay shipping · $7.95 shipping · get my book free · book funnel · author funnel · free copy of my book.

**Signals:** client has/is writing a book or printed asset · goal is a premium buyer list + authority positioning · cold paid traffic with a defined backend (course/coaching/event) · willing to ship a physical product for shipping cost.

**Route elsewhere when:** non-book digital product $27–$97 → SLO Funnel · non-book physical sample → 2-Step Tripwire · just a lead magnet, no printed asset → Lead Magnet Funnel (lead group) · recurring core → Membership/Continuity.

## Page structure (4 pages)

1. **Free-Book 2-Step Order Page** — Sell the free book (pay shipping only) with a 2-step order form: Step 1 captures shipping address (abandoned-cart lead), Step 2 collects shipping payment + the order bump.
   - Hook headline ("Get my book FREE — just cover shipping"); 3D book mockup + what's-inside bullets; author **Epiphany Bridge** story; value framing (book free, you pay only $7.95 shipping); Step 1 name+address; Step 2 payment + **order bump** (audiobook/workbook); testimonials/endorsements + guarantee; scarcity (limited print run).
2. **OTO 1 Page** — One-click upsell (audiobook, companion course, or live training) to cover ad cost.
   - Interrupt headline; upsell VSL/short copy; one-click accept + decline link.
3. **OTO 2 / Downsell Page** — Second upsell or downsell of OTO 1 (payment plan / lite version).
   - One-click accept + decline link.
4. **Confirmation / Bonus Training Page** — Confirm order, deliver high-value bonus training, bridge to the backend.
   - Order/shipping confirmation; high-value **bonus training** delivery; Attractive-Character bonding + authority; high-ticket backend CTA (book a call / apply / next offer).

- **Primary persona — `russell-brunson-the-funnel-hackers-cookbook`** (The Funnel Hacker's Cookbook): governs the free-plus-shipping book recipe — 2-step order, bump + 2 OTOs, bonus-bridge to backend.

- **Supporting — `edwards-copywriting-secrets`** (Copywriting Secrets): headline + benefit-stack formulas, order-bump and OTO microcopy — writes the order page and upsells.
- **Supporting — `brunson-marketing-secrets-blackbook`** (Marketing Secrets Blackbook): Attractive Character authority + Value Ladder — positions the author as the expert and bridges the confirmation-page bonus into the backend.
- **Supporting — `russell-brunson-lead-funnels`** (Lead Funnels): Hook/Story/Offer + unique mechanism — frames the front-end hook and the book's unique-mechanism promise.

The book is a buyer-list builder and authority play, not a profit center on its own.
