# Membership / Continuity Funnel

**id:** `membership-continuity` · **category:** Buyer Funnel · **group:** buyer · **length:** medium-form · **library ref:** #12

**Aliases:** Membership Funnel · Continuity Funnel · Subscription Funnel · Recurring Revenue Funnel · Trial Funnel · Membership Site Funnel

**Source books:** The Funnel Hacker's Cookbook · Marketing Secrets Blackbook · DotCom Secrets

## When to use / trigger criteria

Sells access to a **recurring subscription** — membership site, SaaS, newsletter, or info-product continuity. Often led in with a free trial, $1 trial, or free-plus-shipping box. **Stick rate improves when deployed as a SECOND funnel** after buyers have already bonded with the Attractive Character. A VSL or sales page sells the subscription; member account creation happens on the thank-you page with an automated content drip.

**Goals:** sell recurring/subscription access · add predictable MRR to the value ladder · convert existing buyers into members (stick rate) · onboard and drip content automatically.

**Keywords:** membership · continuity · subscription · recurring · monthly · free trial · $1 trial · membership site · members area · SaaS · newsletter subscription · MRR.

**Signals:** offer is recurring (monthly/annual) · client wants MRR · gated content/software/community to deliver continuously · best deployed to an audience that already bought/bonded.

**Route elsewhere when:** one-time digital product → SLO / Sales Letter / VSL · free/just-shipping physical front end → 2-Step Tripwire (can lead INTO continuity) · deeply discounted one-time deal → Daily Deal · high-ticket coaching → Application Funnel (other group).

---

## Page structure (3 pages + membership area)

1. **Sales / VSL Page (Subscription Offer)** — Sell the recurring membership, framing the ongoing transformation. Lead with a free/$1 trial or free-plus-shipping box to lower the join barrier.
   - Headline framing the ongoing transformation (not a one-time fix); VSL or copy selling subscription value over time; trial-offer framing; **recurring value stack** (what members get each month); cancel-anytime risk reversal + community proof; billing/cancellation FAQ.
2. **Order / Checkout Page (Recurring Billing)** — Collect payment on a recurring schedule (trial logic if used) and an optional bump.
   - Recurring subscription order form (trial → rebill); optional **order bump** (annual prepay / add-on); plan selector (monthly vs annual); trial terms + first-charge date stated.
3. **Member Account Creation / Welcome Thank-You Page** — Create the login, deliver first access, trigger the drip + onboarding.
   - Account/login creation; welcome + first content + quick-start; Attractive-Character onboarding (stick rate); drip schedule + community invite.

Plus: the gated **GHL Membership area** with the content drip.

## Copy framework

- **Primary persona — `brunson-marketing-secrets-blackbook`** (Marketing Secrets Blackbook): governs stick rate — continuity converts best after bonding. The **Soap Opera Sequence** onboards new members; the **Attractive Character** keeps them engaged; **Value Ladder** positions continuity as a rung.
- **Sales script — VSL or Sales Letter selling continuity** (Perfect Webinar or Star/Story/Solution): must sell the recurring transformation over time, not a single deliverable.
- **Supporting — `miller-building-storybrand`** (Building a StoryBrand): SB7 — customer = Hero on a continuing journey; the membership is the Plan/Guide.
- **Supporting — `edwards-copywriting-secrets`** (Copywriting Secrets): trial-offer framing, recurring-value stacking, cancellation/objection copy.
- **Supporting — `miller-coach-builder`** (Coach Builder): when the continuity is a coaching/mastermind membership, structures the entry/core/premium tiers.

Deploy as a **second funnel after buyers bond** for the best stick rate.
