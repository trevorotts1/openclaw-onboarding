# Self-Liquidating Offer (SLO) Funnel

**id:** `self-liquidating-offer-slo` · **category:** Buyer Funnel · **group:** buyer · **length:** medium-form · **library ref:** #9

**Aliases:** SLO Funnel · Self Liquidating Offer · Break-Even Front-End Funnel · Tripwire SLO · Liquidator Funnel

**Source books:** DotCom Secrets

---

## When to use / trigger criteria

A front-end product priced **$27–$97** designed specifically to **break even on cold ad spend** so all upsell revenue is pure profit. A squeeze/lead page leads to the core product via a VSL or long-form sales letter, with an order-form bump and an OTO sequence. Distinct from the tripwire in price point and margin: the SLO carries real margin and aims to recover the full cost of acquiring a customer at the order page.

**Goals:** recover 100% of cold ad spend on the front-end before follow-up runs · sell a $27–$97 info/digital product to cold traffic · build a buyer list while breaking even · fund unlimited paid-traffic scaling.

**Keywords:** self liquidating offer · SLO · break even funnel · $1 in $1 out · $27/$47/$97 offer · front end profit · liquidate ad spend · cold traffic offer.

**Signals:** front-end priced $27–$97 · explicit "pay for itself" / break-even goal · digital/info product (course, toolkit, templates) · cold paid traffic with a defined upsell ladder.

**Route elsewhere when:** front-end is free/just-shipping → 2-Step Tripwire · core product is recurring → Membership/Continuity · front-end is a physical book → Book Funnel.

---

## Page structure (3 pages)

1. **Squeeze / Lead Page** — Capture the email first (curiosity hook or lead magnet) so non-buyers still enter follow-up; redirect opt-ins to the order page.
   - Curiosity headline; single email field (or lead-magnet offer); zero navigation; auto-redirect to step 2.
2. **VSL or Long-Form Sales Letter Order Page** — Sell the $27–$97 core product, convert on an order form with a bump that lifts AOV toward break-even.
   - Big-promise headline; VSL (autoplay + timed CTA reveal) **or** Star/Story/Solution long-form copy; unique-mechanism reveal; value stack with price anchor; **order form with order bump**; guarantee + testimonials + FAQ.
3. **OTO / Upsell Page** — Push AOV above the break-even line with a one-click upsell (higher tier, done-for-you, or bundle).
   - Interrupt headline; upsell VSL/copy with stacked value; one-click accept + decline link; optional downsell on decline.

## Copy framework

- **Primary persona — `russell-brunson-the-funnel-hackers-cookbook`** (The Funnel Hacker's Cookbook): governs SLO structure, the element checklist, and break-even calibration (order-bump + OTO placement).
- **Sales script — Star/Story/Solution (written)** *or* **Perfect Webinar (if VSL)**: pick the modality the audience will consume.
- **Supporting — `brunson-marketing-secrets-blackbook`** (Marketing Secrets Blackbook): Value Ladder positioning + Attractive Character — positions the SLO as rung one and defines the upsell-ladder economics.
- **Supporting — `edwards-copywriting-secrets`** (Copywriting Secrets): headline + benefit-stack formulas, bump/OTO microcopy, price-justification copy — writes the page.
- **Supporting — `miller-building-storybrand`** (Building a StoryBrand): SB7 clarity pass so the offer reads as the customer's transformation, not the product's features.

Pricing + bump + OTO values must be calibrated to hit **$1-in/$1-out** on cold traffic.
