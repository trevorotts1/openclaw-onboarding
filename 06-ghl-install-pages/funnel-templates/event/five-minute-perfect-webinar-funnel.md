# 5-Minute Perfect Webinar

**id:** `five-minute-perfect-webinar-funnel` · **category:** Event Funnels · **library ref:** #34 · **length:** short-form
**aliases:** Five-Minute Perfect Webinar, 5-Min PW VSL, Short Perfect Webinar VSL
**source books:** Expert Secrets

## When to use / trigger criteria
Use as a **sub-5-minute sales video** (origin story + ONE secret + close) for low-ticket (<$100) offers — as ad creative or as the VSL on a tripwire/unboxing/order page with a timed CTA reveal.

- **Goals:** sell a low-ticket impulse offer with a short video; serve as FB/IG ad creative; act as the sales video on a tripwire/unboxing/order page.
- **Keywords:** "5 minute webinar", "short vsl", "quick pitch video", "low ticket", "under $100", "tripwire video", "ad creative", "one secret".
- **Signals:** offer under $100; needs short scroll-stopping video for ads; wants a VSL for a tripwire/unboxing order page; single belief to break fast.
- **Anti-signals:** mid/high-ticket needing full belief work (use Webinar); audience needs deep teaching; multi-speaker/live-event goal (use Summit/Webinar).

## Page structure
1. **Short VSL Sales Page** — big-promise headline; embedded sub-5-min video (origin story → ONE secret → close); timed/delayed CTA reveal after the close; brief proof + guarantee. *Widgets: video embed w/ timed CTA reveal, guarantee block.*
2. **Order Page (with order bump)** — concise stack + price; order form with bump; guarantee + short FAQ; scarcity line. *Widgets: order form + bump, guarantee block, FAQ accordion.*
3. **OTO / Upsell Page (optional)** — one-click upsell + short pitch; yes/no buttons; optional downsell. *Widgets: one-click upsell button, downsell block.*

## Copy framework
- **Primary persona:** `perfect-webinar` — the video is a 5-Minute Perfect Webinar (origin story + ONE secret + close), compressed.
- **Supporting:** `epiphany-bridge` (tight origin story), `copywriting-secrets` (headline, timed-CTA copy, ad hook, order urgency), `star-story-solution` (order-page micro-stack).
- Compression is the skill: one belief, one story, one close, under 5 minutes. The same video doubles as ad creative and on-page VSL.
