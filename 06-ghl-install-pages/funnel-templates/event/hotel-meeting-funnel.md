# Hotel Meeting Funnel (Network Marketing Variant)

**id:** `hotel-meeting-funnel` · **category:** Event Funnels · **library ref:** #37 · **length:** medium-form
**aliases:** Online Hotel Meeting, Opportunity Webinar Funnel, NM Opportunity Funnel, Business Opportunity Presentation Funnel
**source books:** Network Marketing Secrets

## When to use / trigger criteria
Use to **recruit network-marketing business builders online** via an opportunity presentation (live or prerecorded), replacing the offline hotel meeting. Focus is recruiting/duplication, not product sales.

- **Goals:** recruit new business builders/distributors (not product buyers); replace offline hotel meetings; drive a recruiting application / "join my team" CTA via the Viral Loop.
- **Keywords:** "hotel meeting", "opportunity meeting", "business opportunity", "join my team", "opportunity presentation", "network marketing", "recruit", "duplication".
- **Signals:** NM/direct-sales business; goal is recruiting builders; wants a duplicable system distributors can plug into; live or prerecorded opportunity webinar available.
- **Anti-signals:** selling product/course to consumers (use Webinar/Home Party); high-ticket consulting close (use Application Funnel); no recruiting component.

## Page structure
1. **Registration Page** — branded curiosity headline (not company-name-led); what they'll discover (the new opportunity/vehicle); opt-in form; distributor attribution (whose link). *Widgets: opt-in form, distributor attribution field/tag, countdown to presentation.*
2. **Countdown / Confirmation Page** — countdown to start; social proof (team results, leader photos); add-to-calendar + SMS reminders; short "why attend" video. *Widgets: countdown, add-to-calendar, reminder opt-in, social-proof block.*
3. **Live or Auto-Webinar Opportunity Presentation Page** — live/prerecorded opportunity webinar embed; Viral Loop (Epiphany Bridge → demo → call to action); recruiting application / "join my team" CTA (not a product checkout); calendar booking for a sponsor follow-up call. *Widgets: webinar/video embed, recruiting application form, calendar booking, live chat if live.*

## Copy framework
- **Primary persona:** `epiphany-bridge` — the presentation is the Network Marketing Viral Loop (Epiphany Bridge story → demo → call to action).
- **Supporting:** `perfect-webinar` (belief-breaking teaching adapted to the opportunity/new vehicle), `copywriting-secrets` (registration headline + bullets + social proof), `storybrand-sb7` (prospect = hero, sponsor = guide).
- Recruiting, not product selling, is the goal. The CTA is an application / join-my-team / book-a-call, never a product order form. The funnel must be **duplicable** so every distributor runs their own copy with their attribution.

## GHL build notes (Skill 6 + Skill 44)
- **Skill 6** builds the 3 pages with the opportunity webinar embed.
- **Skill 44 widgets:** registration form with distributor attribution; countdown + add-to-calendar + reminders; social-proof block; recruiting application form (multi-field); calendar booking for the sponsor call.
- **Automations:** register → reminders + presentation access; attribution tag routes leads to the correct distributor/sponsor; application submit → notify sponsor + confirm booking; no-show → replay + re-invite.
- **Products:** no product checkout — recruiting application / team enrollment is the conversion. Build as a duplicable template every distributor can clone with their own attribution.
