# Product Launch Funnel

**id:** `product-launch-funnel` · **category:** Event Funnels · **library ref:** #17 · **length:** long-form
**aliases:** Jeff Walker Launch Funnel, PLF, Sideways Perfect Webinar, Pre-Launch Sequence Funnel
**source books:** Cookbook, DotCom Secrets, Expert Secrets · **origin:** Jeff Walker

## When to use / trigger criteria
Use for a launch event to a warm list/audience where anticipation is built over several days with sequential content videos before a hard cart-open/cart-close window.

- **Goals:** build anticipation + reciprocity with free pre-launch content; deliver the Perfect Webinar "sideways" across 3 videos; create a launch spike with hard cart windows.
- **Keywords:** "product launch", "launch funnel", "pre-launch", "PLF", "cart opens", "free video series", "video 1/2/3", "doors open".
- **Signals:** warm list to launch to; wants an event-style spike not always-on; offer benefits from anticipation + comment social proof; time to release content over days.
- **Anti-signals:** cold traffic with no relationship; always-on need (use AutoWebinar); no bandwidth for 3–4 videos.

## Page structure
1. **PLC #1 — "Wow and How" (Opportunity)** — opt-in gate; Video 1 = big idea / new opportunity / why now; comment section; "Video 2 in X days" CTA. *Widgets: opt-in form, video embed, comment block.*
2. **PLC #2 — "Transformational Education" (Process)** — Video 2 = the transformation + step-by-step process/case study; recap + answer top comments; "Video 3 + offer coming" CTA. *Widgets: video embed, comment block.*
3. **PLC #3 — "Ownership Experience" (Result + Cart Tease)** — Video 3 = future-pace ownership; objection handling; cart-open date + waitlist tease. *Widgets: video embed, waitlist form, comment block, countdown-to-cart-open.*
4. **Cart-Open Offer Page (Video 4 + Order)** — sales video w/ the Stack + price; order form with bump; cart-close countdown (hard deadline); guarantee + expiring bonuses + FAQ. *Widgets: sales video, order form + bump, cart-close countdown, FAQ accordion.*

## Copy framework
- **Primary persona:** `epiphany-bridge` — Videos 1–3 spread the story "sideways" (opportunity → process → ownership).
- **Supporting:** `perfect-webinar` (Video 4 = the Stack & Close), `copywriting-secrets` (video hooks, comment prompts, subject lines), `star-story-solution` (order-page reinforcement).
- It is literally a "sideways Perfect Webinar": Wow-and-How = hook + vehicle belief; Transformational Education = internal/external belief breaks; Ownership Experience = future-pace; Video 4 = the Stack and close.
