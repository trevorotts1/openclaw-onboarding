# Ask Campaign Funnel

**id:** `ask-campaign`  |  **category:** Lead Funnels  |  **skill:** Skill 6  |  **length:** short-form

**Aliases:** Deep-Dive Survey, Voice-of-Customer Funnel

**Source (master library entry #6):** The Funnel Hacker's Cookbook

## When To Use / Trigger Criteria

A single open-ended question - typically 'What is your #1 challenge/question with [topic]?' - deployed to discover what customers actually want. Responses guide product creation and reveal the false beliefs to overcome in sales copy. Exploratory and research-driven, NOT segmentation-driven.

**Goals it matches:**
- Market research
- Discover customer language & false beliefs
- Validate before building a product
- Gather voice-of-customer for future copy

**Keywords / request shapes:**
`survey my audience`, `what do they want`, `research`, `biggest challenge`, `ask my list`, `voice of customer`, `validate idea`, `what to build`

**Signals:**
- You are pre-product or pre-copy
- You need real customer language
- You want to surface the objections to overcome later

**Do NOT use when:**
- You are ready to sell now (use a sales/lead funnel)
- You need segmentation/routing (use Survey/Quiz)

## Page Structure

### Page 1: Ask Question Page
*Purpose:* Capture one unbiased open-ended answer.

Key blocks:
- Short framing headline
- Single open text-area ('What is your #1 question/challenge with ___?')
- Name + Email fields
- Submit button
- Deliberately minimal copy

### Page 2: Thank-You Page
*Purpose:* Close the loop and softly pre-frame a future offer.

Key blocks:
- Thank-you message
- 'Watch this while you wait' video or relevant resource
- Optional soft offer/opt-in
- Responses piped to a reporting view/spreadsheet

## Copy Framework

**Primary persona (writes the copy):** The Funnel Architect (Russell Brunson, DotCom Secrets)

**Script:** Single open-ended question with deliberately minimal copy so answers stay unbiased. No selling on Page 1 (exploratory voice rooted in the Ask method).

**Copy structure / beats:**
- One-line warm frame
- The question
- Submit
- (Thank-You may pre-frame a future offer)

**Notes:** Exploratory, not segmentation. The gold is the raw answers - mine them for headlines, hooks, and the false beliefs to break in later funnels.

## GoHighLevel Build Notes (Skill 6 + Skill 44)

**Skill 44 widgets (forms / calendars / interactive):**

**Automations:**
- Tag 'ask-respondent'
- Notify owner of new responses
- Aggregate responses for analysis

**Build notes:** Export responses to a sheet and categorize answers into belief buckets. Keep the page sparse to avoid biasing the answers.
