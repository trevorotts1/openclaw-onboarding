# Bridge Funnel

**id:** `bridge`  |  **category:** Lead Funnels  |  **skill:** Skill 6  |  **length:** medium-form

**Aliases:** Presell Page Funnel, Cold Traffic Article Pre-Frame Funnel

**Source (master library entry #4):** The Funnel Hacker's Cookbook, Marketing Secrets Blackbook, Network Marketing Secrets, DotCom Secrets, Traffic Secrets

## When To Use / Trigger Criteria

Pre-frame and connect two related ideas before forwarding the visitor to a destination funnel the marketer may not own. Page 1 captures the email; Page 2 tells an Epiphany Bridge story/video and redirects to the main offer. Used by affiliates, network marketers, and anyone sending cold traffic that needs warming first.

**Goals it matches:**
- Warm cold traffic before an offer
- Presell an affiliate / partner / network-marketing offer
- Connect two ideas / bridge a belief gap
- Capture the email before handing off

**Keywords / request shapes:**
`affiliate`, `network marketing`, `presell`, `bridge`, `warm up cold traffic`, `promote someone else's offer`, `downline`, `pre-frame`

**Signals:**
- You are sending traffic to an offer you do NOT control
- Audience is not yet sold on the vehicle
- A personal story is needed to connect the two ideas

**Do NOT use when:**
- You own the offer and traffic is already warm (go direct)
- There is no belief gap to bridge

## Page Structure

### Page 1: Curiosity Squeeze Page
*Purpose:* Capture the email before the bridge so the lead is yours even if they bounce at the destination.

Key blocks:
- Curiosity headline tied to the bridge promise
- Single Email field
- CTA
- No navigation

### Page 2: Bridge Page
*Purpose:* Tell the Epiphany Bridge story (usually on video) and hand the warmed prospect off to the destination.

Key blocks:
- Embedded pre-sell story video
- Supporting transcript/copy
- The 'here's what I found' reveal
- Outbound redirect button to the destination offer
- Affiliate / disclosure note

### Page 1-alt: Cold Traffic Article Pre-Frame Page (variant)
*Purpose:* Replaces Page 1 for fully cold traffic - a native article written at the audience's awareness level that ends in the opt-in.

Key blocks:
- Native-article headline in the audience's language
- Story-led body bridging beliefs to the solution
- Opt-in CTA entering a 30-60 day cold-to-warm nurture

## Copy Framework

**Primary persona (writes the copy):** The Expert Guide (Russell Brunson, Expert Secrets)

**Supporting personas:** The Traffic Strategist (Russell Brunson, Traffic Secrets)

**Script:** Epiphany Bridge Script (Expert Secrets) - the canonical home of the Epiphany Bridge.

**Copy structure / beats:**
- Hook
- Personal backstory & journey
- The epiphany that led me to [the vehicle]
- The framework / what I found
- Achievement & transformation
- 'Click here to see it for yourself' hand-off

**Notes:** The bridge transfers YOUR belief in the vehicle to the prospect, so the destination offer (which you may not own) converts a pre-sold visitor.

## GoHighLevel Build Notes (Skill 6 + Skill 44)

**Funnel pages (Skill 6 instantiates):**
- Step 1: Curiosity squeeze page
- Step 2: Bridge page with outbound redirect

**Skill 44 widgets (forms / calendars / interactive):**
- Opt-in Form widget on page 1
- Video embed on page 2
- Outbound link button (destination/affiliate URL with UTM + affiliate params)

**Automations:**
- Tag 'bridge-lead'
- Nurture sequence reusing the Epiphany Bridge
- Retarget non-clickers

**Build notes:** The redirect button opens the destination (often external). Pass tracking parameters. For cold traffic, swap Page 1 for the article pre-frame variant.
