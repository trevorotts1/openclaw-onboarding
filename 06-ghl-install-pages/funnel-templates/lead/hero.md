# Hero Funnel

**id:** `hero`  |  **category:** Lead Funnels  |  **skill:** Skill 6  |  **length:** short-form

**Aliases:** Personal Brand Funnel, Origin Story Funnel, Attractive Character Funnel

**Source (master library entry #18):** The Funnel Hacker's Cookbook

## When To Use / Trigger Criteria

Showcases the entrepreneur's biography and origin story to build an email list and drive social media follows. Typically linked from email signatures and social bios. The thank-you page delivers the first email and pushes follow buttons - the personal-brand front door.

**Goals it matches:**
- Build a personal-brand email list
- Grow social following
- Introduce the Attractive Character
- Capture from email signature / social bios

**Keywords / request shapes:**
`about me`, `my story`, `personal brand`, `follow me`, `bio link`, `origin story`, `build my audience`, `influencer`, `link in bio`

**Signals:**
- The business is personal-brand-led
- You are linking from a social bio / email signature
- You want follows AND email together

**Do NOT use when:**
- The business is product-led with no personal brand (use Homepage/Storefront)
- You need a direct-response sale (use a sales funnel)

## Page Structure

### Page 1: Hero Story / Bio Opt-in Page
*Purpose:* Tell the origin story and capture the email.

Key blocks:
- Hero photo
- Origin narrative (Attractive Character backstory + flaws + polarity)
- What you stand for / your mission
- Opt-in form for the 'first email' or a free thing
- Social proof

### Page 2: Thank-You + Follow Page
*Purpose:* Deliver the first email and push social follows.

Key blocks:
- Confirmation
- Social follow buttons (all platforms)
- 'Here's where to find me'
- First Soap Opera email trigger

## Copy Framework

**Primary persona (writes the copy):** The Funnel Architect (Russell Brunson, DotCom Secrets)

**Supporting personas:** The Expert Guide (Russell Brunson, Expert Secrets); The Story Brander (Donald Miller, Building a StoryBrand (StoryBrand SB7 / BrandScript))

**Script:** The Attractive Character (DotCom Secrets: backstory, parables, character flaws, polarizing stances) told as an Epiphany Bridge (Expert Secrets); StoryBrand framing positions you as the Guide who was once the hero.

**Copy structure / beats:**
- Relatable origin
- Struggle
- Epiphany
- Mission
- 'Follow / join me'

**Notes:** Authenticity plus polarity build connection. The goal is a relationship (list + follows), not an immediate sale.
