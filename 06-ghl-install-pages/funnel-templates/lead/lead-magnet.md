# Lead Magnet Funnel

**id:** `lead-magnet`  |  **category:** Lead Funnels  |  **skill:** Skill 6  |  **length:** short-form

**Aliases:** Epiphany Lead Funnel, Lead Funnel

**Source (master library entry #3):** The Funnel Hacker's Cookbook, Lead Funnels (Swipe File), DotCom Secrets, Expert Secrets, Traffic Secrets

## When To Use / Trigger Criteria

Exchange a tangible deliverable (ebook, report, checklist, cheat sheet, swipe file, video, or coupon) directly for an email on a dedicated offer page. The lead magnet is the hook; the thank-you page begins relationship-building and routes the lead into a follow-up sequence.

**Goals it matches:**
- List-building with a specific named asset
- Front-end lead generation
- Route leads into a follow-up sequence
- Value-stack a free deliverable

**Keywords / request shapes:**
`free ebook`, `checklist`, `cheat sheet`, `download`, `free report`, `template`, `swipe file`, `lead magnet`, `guide`

**Signals:**
- You have a specific concrete deliverable
- You want to value-stack the freebie
- You want delivery + automated follow-up

**Do NOT use when:**
- You have no concrete deliverable yet (use Squeeze with a promise)
- Cold traffic needs warming first (use Bridge)

## Page Structure

### Page 1: Lead Magnet Offer Page
*Purpose:* Sell the free thing as if it were a paid product so perceived value dwarfs 'an email.'

Key blocks:
- Benefit headline naming the deliverable
- Hero image / 3D mockup of the asset
- Value-stack bullets (what's inside + perceived $ value)
- Opt-in form (Name optional + Email)
- Single CTA
- Social proof

### Page 2: Thank-You / Delivery Page
*Purpose:* Deliver the asset and begin the relationship.

Key blocks:
- Download / access button
- 'Check your email' instruction
- Indoctrination / bridge video
- Next-step CTA into the value ladder
- Soap Opera Sequence trigger

## Copy Framework

**Primary persona (writes the copy):** The Copy Closer (Jim Edwards, Copywriting Secrets)

**Supporting personas:** The Funnel Architect (Russell Brunson, DotCom Secrets)

**Script:** Hook-Story-Offer (DotCom Secrets) wrapped around Jim Edwards' fascination-bullet formula for the 'what's inside' section.

**Copy structure / beats:**
- Hook = result headline
- Micro-story = why this matters now
- Offer = the value-stacked free deliverable
- 3-5 fascination bullets
- Mockup
- CTA
- Reassurance

**Notes:** Treat the free lead magnet like a paid product - a full value stack so perceived value vastly exceeds the cost of an email address.
