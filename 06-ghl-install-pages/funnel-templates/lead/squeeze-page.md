# Squeeze Page Funnel

**id:** `squeeze-page`  |  **category:** Lead Funnels  |  **skill:** Skill 6  |  **length:** short-form

**Aliases:** Opt-in Funnel, Lead Squeeze Funnel

**Source (master library entry #1):** The Funnel Hacker's Cookbook, Marketing Secrets Blackbook, Lead Funnels (Swipe File), DotCom Secrets, Expert Secrets, Network Marketing Secrets, Traffic Secrets

## When To Use / Trigger Criteria

Fastest, lowest-friction way to capture an email from warm-to-medium traffic by trading a single curiosity-driven promise for the opt-in. The most foundational lead-capture mechanism in the Brunson system.

**Goals it matches:**
- Grow an email list quickly
- Capture leads at the lowest cost per lead
- Stand up the front-end of a value ladder
- Gate content behind an opt-in

**Keywords / request shapes:**
`opt-in`, `email capture`, `lead capture`, `grow my list`, `subscribe`, `squeeze page`, `free guide (single field)`, `join the list`

**Signals:**
- Traffic is already somewhat aware
- One simple promise is enough
- No need to teach or warm the visitor first
- You want the absolute minimum friction to capture

**Do NOT use when:**
- Traffic is cold/skeptical and needs warming (use Bridge or Reverse Squeeze)
- Offer is high-ticket and needs qualification (use Application)
- You need to segment buyers (use Survey/Quiz)

## Page Structure

### Page 1: Squeeze / Opt-in Page
*Purpose:* Trade a curiosity-driven promise for the visitor's email before they can proceed.

Key blocks:
- Curiosity headline (the entire conversion lever)
- Optional one-line sub-headline
- Single Email field + submit button
- Zero navigation / no header links / no distractions
- 3 supporting curiosity bullets (optional)
- Privacy reassurance line
- Optional background image or muted video

### Page 2: Thank-You / Delivery Page
*Purpose:* Deliver the promise and set the next step; auto-redirect destination of the opt-in.

Key blocks:
- Confirmation message
- Access link or 'check your email' instruction
- Indoctrination/bridge video or next-step CTA
- Soap Opera Sequence trigger

## Copy Framework

**Primary persona (writes the copy):** The Copy Closer (Jim Edwards, Copywriting Secrets)

**Supporting personas:** The Funnel Architect (Russell Brunson, DotCom Secrets)

**Script:** Curiosity-Gap Headline formula + the compressed 'Hook' of Hook-Story-Offer (story and offer are deferred to the follow-up).

**Copy structure / beats:**
- Headline using the 4 U's (Useful, Urgent, Unique, Ultra-specific)
- Single curiosity bullet/promise
- One CTA

**Notes:** One promise, one field, one button. The squeeze carries no story on the page; its entire job is the headline hook. A/B test headlines relentlessly.

**Automations:**

- Trigger Soap Opera email sequence

**Build notes:** Strip the funnel header so there is zero navigation. Use a sticky single CTA. Keep the form to Email only to minimize friction.
