## When To Use / Trigger Criteria
**Goals (request shapes that should match):**
- Recover 100% of cold ad spend on the front end so paid traffic can scale 'unlimited' ($1 in -> $1 out, all backend = profit).
- Convert a leaky front-end funnel into a self-liquidating one by adding/tuning order bumps and OTOs.

**Keywords:** `break even`, `breakeven`, `self liquidating`, `SLO`, `recover ad spend`, `scale paid traffic`, `$1 in $1 out`, `front-end profitable`, `free customer acquisition`, `order bump`, `OTO math`

**Signals:**
- Client is (or wants to be) running cold paid traffic and is losing money on the front end.
- There is already a value ladder / backend, so front-end profit isn't required — break-even is enough to scale.
- Client asks 'how do I make my ads profitable / scale without going broke'.

**Anti-signals (do NOT match / route elsewhere):**
- Pure organic/free-traffic business with no ad spend to recover — break-even math is moot.
- No upsell inventory exists yet — build the value-ladder offers first.

## Page Structure
### 1. Front-End Acquisition Page (base funnel)
*Purpose:* Acquire the buyer cheaply via the chosen base funnel: squeeze->SLO sales page, 2-step tripwire, free-plus-shipping book, or webinar registration.

*Key elements / blocks:*
- Base-funnel hero (VSL or sales letter or registration per chosen type)
- Low-friction front-end price point ($0 F+S to $97 SLO)
- Single clear CTA into the order/registration step

### 2. 2-Step Order Form + Order Bump
*Purpose:* Capture lead on step 1 (recover abandons), bill on step 2, and lift AOV with an order bump — the first break-even lever.

*Key elements / blocks:*
- Step 1: contact info (captures abandoned-cart leads)
- Step 2: billing + a checkbox ORDER BUMP (complementary, high-margin)
- Edwards-style bump copy ('Add ___ for just $X — yes, add it to my order')

### 3. OTO 1 (Profit-Maximizer Upsell)
*Purpose:* First one-click upsell that pushes the funnel to break-even on cold traffic.

*Key elements / blocks:*
- One-click upsell (no re-enter payment)
- The Stack restated for the upsell
- Yes/No decision links only

### 4. OTO 2 / Downsell
*Purpose:* Second upsell or a downsell for those who declined OTO 1 — final break-even lever.

*Key elements / blocks:*
- Higher-tier upsell OR downsell of OTO 1 at lower price/payment plan
- One-click accept/decline

### 5. Confirmation / Bridge Page
*Purpose:* Confirm the order and bridge the new buyer to the backend value ladder (where the real profit lives).

*Key elements / blocks:*
- Order summary + delivery/access
- High-value bonus or next-step CTA into the backend offer
- Follow-up sequence trigger

## Metrics To Calibrate
- Cost per acquisition (CPA) vs front-end revenue per buyer
- Order-bump take rate
- OTO1/OTO2 take rates
- Average order value (AOV)
- Break-even ratio (revenue / ad spend) >= 1.0
