# ANALYSIS NOTES - The New Model of Selling (Acuff & Miner)

**Slug:** acuff-miner-new-model-selling
**Phase:** 2 (Analysis) - 12 dimensions

---

## 1. Amateur-to-Expert Gap (6 dimensions)
| Dimension | Amateur (Old Model) | Expert (NEPQ / New Model) |
|---|---|---|
| Opening | Name + company + reason-for-call pitch | Connecting question (focus on prospect) |
| Talk ratio | Salesperson dominates | Prospect talks most of engagement |
| Conversation goal | Make the sale | Discover whether a sale exists |
| Problem ownership | Seller asserts the problem | Prospect names problem + cost in own words |
| Objections | Handled at the close | Prevented in engagement |
| Tonality / close | Enthusiastic + Always Be Closing | Calm/neutral + Always Be Disarming, "commit" |

## 2. Failure Taxonomy
Lost-at-Hello (pitch opener), Premature Presenting, Feature Dump, Pressure Languaging ("close/sign/buy now"), Assumptive-Close Objection Whack-a-Mole, Surface Acceptance (no probing), Enthusiasm Overload, Numbers-Game Grind, Single-Option Proposal, Emailing the proposal before presenting it.

## 3. Execution Standard (measurable quality bar)
A conversation/script is "New Model-grade" when: opens with a connecting question; engagement (questions) is the bulk of the interaction; presentation is ~10% and mirrors only the 2-3 stated problems with three options at ~10x value-to-cost; commitment is ~5% and uses "commit" + a "Why though?" self-persuasion follow-up; all pressure/assumptive language is replaced with neutral languaging; objections are prevented, not handled.

## 4. Decision Rules (8+)
1. Pitch in the opener -> replace with a connecting question.
2. Objection appears -> return to engagement questions (don't rebut).
3. Many features listed -> cut to the 2-3 stated problems.
4. "close/sign/buy now" present -> swap to neutral languaging.
5. Surface answer given -> add a clarifying question.
6. No urgency -> add a consequence (3/6/12-month) question.
7. Budget unknown -> qualify before presenting.
8. Hype tone -> shift to calm/neutral/slow.
9. Single offer -> provide three options.
10. Asking for the sale early -> hold commitment to the final 5%.

## 5. Department / Role Application Map
- **Sales:** Closer, Account Executive, Chief Sales Officer -> discovery + commitment scripts.
- **CRM / Convert-and-Flow:** CRM Automation Workflow Specialist, Appointment Setter/SDR -> qualifying flows, nurture sequences.

- **Leadership/Coaching (founder):** founder-led selling and rep training.

## 7. Voice & Tonality Profile
Calm "trusted teacher" (Acuff) + behavioral-science "sales Jedi" (Miner). Neutral, curious, slightly playful, never hype. Scripted-but-natural (actor analogy). Slow pace, empathy, nodding/mirroring even on Zoom.
