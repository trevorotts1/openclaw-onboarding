## Dimension 1 — The Amateur-to-Expert Gap (6 dimensions)

| Dimension | Amateur (stuck in the phases) | Expert (escaped via funnels) |
|---|---|---|
| Selling logic | Leads with science/ingredients (technobabble) | Leads with the Epiphany Bridge story |
| Audience | Pitches product-lovers + builders at once | One dream client per funnel |
| Buying path | Hands prospect to the corporate "anti-sales" site | Pre-framing funnel into checkout |
| Lead source | Friends, family, social spam (finite pond) | Funnel + assigned traffic sources |
| Income model | One-time contest spike (Ferrari, no residual) | Residual via Duplication + replay |
| Team growth | Personal hustle / cloned company template | Duplicable system + pattern interrupt |

The gap is not talent or effort — top distributors in the book were already working hard. The gap is *model*: amateurs optimize personal selling; experts engineer a duplicable funnel that sells and recruits without them.

## Dimension 2 — Failure Taxonomy
1. **Technobabble Pitch** — logic-first copy that repels (the #1 sales killer).
2. **Two-Avatar Mush** — one funnel selling both product and opportunity → diluted, no buyers.
3. **Anti-Sales Handoff** — warm lead dies on the corporate cart.
4. **Clone-War Sameness** — identical company template ignored by the market.
5. **Ferrari-No-Residual** — big spike, no Duplication underneath, income craters.
6. **Website-Not-Funnel** — many choices/links, no single ordered path.
7. **Empty-Funnel** — no traffic source assigned; the car has no fuel.
8. **Premature Scaling** — building three funnels at once instead of mastering one.

## Dimension 3 — Execution Standard (what "excellent" means here)
A deliverable is excellent when it is: a single-avatar funnel; story-led (Epiphany Bridge first); a complete Viral Loop (Bridge → Demo → CTA → Duplication); a path of ordered pages + emails; protective of the prospect through checkout (no anti-sales handoff); duplicable by a brand-new recruit (Level 2/3 wired); and fueled by 2+ named traffic sources.

## Dimension 4 — Decision Rules (8+)
1. Copy opens with science → rewrite to lead with the Epiphany Bridge.
2. Two avatars in one funnel → split into two funnels.
3. Prospect sent to corporate site → insert a pre-framing funnel + show the order form.
4. Using the company's template → add a pattern interrupt / custom funnel.
5. Income is a one-time spike → wire Level-2/3 Duplication.
6. Rep out of warm market → build a funnel and turn on traffic.
7. Others will promote the funnel → swap the raw signup link for "contact your referrer" / share-funnel.
8. Nervous about closing → use the permission-ask and repeat the CTA.
9. Live event ended → re-date registration and keep the replay live.
10. Building from scratch → master one funnel before adding a second.
