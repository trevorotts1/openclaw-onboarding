# Suggested Roles — account-management-dept
**Version:** 1.0.0 | June 2026
**Status:** v1.0.0 canonical reconciliation — roster, role-library `_index.json`, and dept-scoped instantiation agree on **6 roles**. Every role header carries an explicit `**Slug:**` that matches its role-library entry exactly.

## Department Purpose
Own post-sale client relationships from handoff through renewal. Drive retention, expansion, and referrals. Serve as the strategic partner to accounts, ensuring they achieve outcomes, renew, and expand. Interface between client needs and internal delivery teams.

## Roles

### 0. Director of Account Management
**Slug:** director-of-account-management
**Role type:** director
**What it does:** Strategic oversight of account management. Sets retention, net revenue retention, and expansion KPIs. Manages the account team, coordinates with Sales and Customer Support, and reports to CEO on account health.
**Core SOPs to build:**
- 01-How-to-Run-an-Account-Management-Standup.md
- 02-How-to-Report-Retention-and-NRR-KPIs.md
- 03-How-to-Manage-At-Risk-Accounts.md
- 04-How-to-Coordinate-With-Sales-on-Expansions.md
**Persona Trait Suggestions:** Relationship-first, retention-obsessed, expansion-minded, strategic.
