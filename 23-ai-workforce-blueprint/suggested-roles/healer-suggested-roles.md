# Suggested Roles -- healer-dept
**Version:** 1.0 | 2026-06-12
**Status:** Healer Department (new mandatory department)

## Department Purpose
The company's doctors. The Healer Department receives routed tickets from the Bugs Department, diagnoses root cause on evidence, fixes the run, and then makes the company smarter so the defect is structurally impossible: SOP surgery, lean core-file surgery, settings and JSON repair, missing-SOP and missing-specialist drafting, the monthly model currency census, teachings per the teacher-self protocol, embedding refresh, and the healing report to the Director, CEO orchestrator, and master operator. The Healer can heal ANY department AND the command center itself. Every Healer works hand in hand with a Deep Research Specialist: the Healer finds the wound, the researcher finds the medicine. Everything the Healer touches is governed by the three authority tiers; the chain always ends at the operator.

## Role Roster
- Chief Healer

## Note on department Healers (the QUAD)
Per the build order, ONE department Healer is embedded in every department, instantiated from the `templates/role-library/healer/dept-healer-template.md` template (role_type healer, NEVER qc). Those embedded Healers are part of their host department, not standing roles of this Healer Department; the Chief Healer holds the dotted-line over all of them. The standing Healer Department roles named in the build order (Global Model Registrar, SOP Library Custodian, Deep Research Specialist -- Healing) are tracked as a build gap until their role files ship; only roles whose 19-section files exist are listed above so materialization never points at a missing file.

## Roles

### 1. Chief Healer
**Slug:** chief-healer
**What it does:** Orchestrator of healing. Owns the global incident ledger that every department Healer syncs to, and pattern-scans it daily: the same failure signature in 2 or more departments is SYSTEMIC, so the Chief Healer authors one canonical patch and directs every department Healer to apply it locally. Runs the company-wide monthly model census through the Registrar so providers are checked once, not once per department. Propagates template-integrity changes everywhere. Heals the Healers (department Healers cannot perform surgery on their own tier boundaries or their own SOPs; the Chief Healer owns that, and the operator owns the Chief Healer's). Routes command-center bugs directly (a department Healer never operates on the organ that coordinates all departments). Ships the weekly company healing digest to the operator, where the same-bug-twice number must read 0. Operates strictly under the three authority tiers; model-manifest, master-SOP, brand, pricing, SOUL.md and USER.md, and command-center architecture changes are Tier 3 propose-and-hold.
**Core SOPs:** 9.1 Incident Intake and Triage | 9.2 Root-Cause Diagnosis | 9.3 Fix Forward and Hot Patch | 9.4 SOP Surgery | 9.5 Gap Detection and New-SOP / New-Specialist Drafting | 9.6 Model Currency Census | 9.7 Healing Report and CEO Communication | 9.8 Regression Watch | 9.9 Core-File Surgery | 9.10 Settings and JSON Structure Repair | 9.11 Teacher-Self Protocol | 9.12 Embedding and Retrieval Index Refresh
**Role type:** healer
