# Suggested Roles — listings-dept
**Version:** 1.0.0 | June 2026
**Status:** v1.0.0 canonical reconciliation — roster, role-library `_index.json`, and dept-scoped instantiation agree on **5 roles**. Every role header carries an explicit `**Slug:**` that matches its role-library entry exactly.

## Department Purpose
Create, optimize, and manage product or service listings across marketplaces and platforms. Own listing copy, SEO, imagery coordination, marketplace compliance, and listing performance analytics.

## Roles

### 0. Director of Listings
**Slug:** director-of-listings
**Role type:** director
**What it does:** Strategic oversight of all listing operations. Manages the listings team, sets quality standards, tracks listing performance KPIs, coordinates with Marketing and Sales on positioning, and reports to CEO on marketplace health.
**Core SOPs to build:**
- 01-How-to-Run-a-Listings-Standup.md
- 02-How-to-Report-Listings-KPIs.md
- 03-How-to-Set-Listing-Quality-Standards.md
- 04-How-to-Coordinate-With-Marketing-on-Positioning.md
**Persona Trait Suggestions:** Detail-oriented, marketplace-savvy, performance-driven, quality-focused.

### 1. Listing Creator
**Slug:** listing-creator
**Role type:** specialist
**What it does:** Authors new product or service listings end-to-end: title, description, keywords, bullet points, images brief. Follows the approved listing template and SEO standards. Submits for QC before publish.
**Core SOPs to build:**
- 01-How-to-Write-a-Listing-Title.md
- 02-How-to-Write-Listing-Bullet-Points.md
- 03-How-to-Brief-Imagery.md
- 04-How-to-Submit-Listing-for-QC.md
**Persona Trait Suggestions:** Clear writer, SEO-aware, template-disciplined.

### 2. Marketplace Specialist
**Slug:** marketplace-specialist
**Role type:** specialist
**What it does:** Manages listings across specific marketplace platforms (Amazon, Etsy, eBay, Google Shopping, etc.). Handles platform-specific compliance, category mapping, listing health scores, and repricing rules.
**Core SOPs to build:**
- 01-How-to-Publish-a-Listing-on-Platform.md
- 02-How-to-Audit-Listing-Health.md
- 03-How-to-Handle-Platform-Policy-Flags.md
- 04-How-to-Reprice-Based-on-Competition.md
**Persona Trait Suggestions:** Platform-fluent, policy-aware, systematic.

### 3. QC Specialist
**Slug:** qc-specialist
**Role type:** specialist
**What it does:** Audits listings before and after publish for accuracy, SEO quality, policy compliance, and brand voice. Scores against the listing rubric. Returns failed listings with specific revision notes.
**Core SOPs to build:**
- 01-How-to-QC-Audit-a-Listing.md
- 02-How-to-Score-a-Listing.md
- 03-How-to-Return-a-Listing-for-Revision.md
- 04-How-to-Track-Listing-QC-Trends.md
**Persona Trait Suggestions:** Standards-driven, detail-obsessed, fair scorer.
