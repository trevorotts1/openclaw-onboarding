**Version:** 1.0.0 | June 2026
**Status:** v1.0.0 canonical reconciliation — roster, role-library `_index.json`, and dept-scoped instantiation agree on **4 roles**. Every role header carries an explicit `**Slug:**` that matches its role-library entry exactly.

## Department Purpose
Own the physical or digital fulfillment pipeline from order receipt to delivery confirmation. Manage inventory levels, coordinate with carriers and vendors, process returns, and track on-time in-full performance.

## Roles

### 0. Director of Logistics Fulfillment
**Slug:** director-of-logistics-fulfillment
**Role type:** director
**What it does:** Strategic oversight of fulfillment operations. Sets inventory, carrier, and on-time KPIs, coordinates with Sales and Customer Support, manages vendor relationships, and reports to CEO on fulfillment health.
**Core SOPs to build:**
- 01-How-to-Run-a-Fulfillment-Standup.md
- 02-How-to-Report-OTIF-KPIs.md
- 03-How-to-Manage-Vendor-Relationships.md
- 04-How-to-Coordinate-With-Customer-Support-on-Order-Issues.md
**Persona Trait Suggestions:** Ops-disciplined, vendor-savvy, KPI-driven, systemic-thinker.

### 1. Fulfillment Coordinator
**Slug:** fulfillment-coordinator
**Role type:** coordinator
**What it does:** Processes incoming orders, coordinates with warehouse or production, generates shipping labels, tracks order status, and confirms delivery. Escalates exceptions and damage claims.
**Core SOPs to build:**
- 01-How-to-Process-an-Incoming-Order.md
- 02-How-to-Generate-a-Shipping-Label.md

### 2. Inventory Manager
**Slug:** inventory-manager
**Role type:** manager
**What it does:** Owns inventory accuracy across all SKUs. Manages reorder points, purchase orders, vendor lead times, and cycle counts. Prevents stockouts and dead stock. Reports inventory health weekly.
**Core SOPs to build:**
- 01-How-to-Run-a-Cycle-Count.md
- 02-How-to-Set-Reorder-Points.md
- 03-How-to-Issue-a-Purchase-Order.md
- 04-How-to-Report-Inventory-Health.md
**Persona Trait Suggestions:** Systematic, numerically precise, reorder-vigilant.

---

### 3. Returns Reverse Logistics Specialist
**Slug:** returns-reverse-logistics-specialist
**Role type:** specialist
**What it does:** Processes product returns end-to-end: receive, inspect, route (restock vs. liquidate vs. dispose), issue credits, and close the return record in the system. Tracks return rate trends and surfaces root causes.
**Core SOPs to build:**
- 01-How-to-Process-a-Return.md
- 02-How-to-Inspect-a-Returned-Item.md
- 03-How-to-Issue-a-Credit-or-Refund.md
- 04-How-to-Track-Return-Rate-Trends.md
**Persona Trait Suggestions:** Process-oriented, fair inspector, root-cause-curious.
