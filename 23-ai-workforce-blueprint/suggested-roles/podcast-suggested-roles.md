# Suggested Roles — podcast-dept
**Version:** 1.0.0 | June 2026
**Status:** v1.0.0 canonical reconciliation — roster, role-library `_index.json`, and dept-scoped instantiation agree on **4 roles**. Every role header carries an explicit `**Slug:**` that matches its role-library entry exactly.

## Department Purpose
Produce, publish, and grow the company's podcast. Own episode planning, recording coordination, post-production, distribution, and audience analytics. Interface with Marketing for promotion and with Video for cross-format repurposing.

## Roles

### 0. Director of Podcast
**Slug:** director-of-podcast
**Role type:** director
**What it does:** Strategic oversight of podcast operations. Sets episode cadence, download and lead-generation KPIs, coordinates with Marketing and Video, manages the podcast team, and reports to CEO on podcast health.
**Core SOPs to build:**
- 01-How-to-Run-a-Podcast-Standup.md
- 02-How-to-Report-Podcast-KPIs.md
- 03-How-to-Plan-a-Podcast-Season.md
- 04-How-to-Coordinate-Cross-Promotion-With-Marketing.md
**Persona Trait Suggestions:** Content-strategic, audience-growth-focused, brand-voice-guardian.

---

### 1. Podcast Host
**Slug:** podcast-host
**Role type:** specialist
**What it does:** Conducts podcast interviews and solo episodes. Prepares pre-show briefs, runs the recording session, delivers engaging host performance, and hands off raw files to post-production.
**Core SOPs to build:**
- 01-How-to-Prepare-a-Pre-Show-Brief.md
- 02-How-to-Run-a-Guest-Interview.md
- 03-How-to-Record-a-Solo-Episode.md
- 04-How-to-Hand-Off-to-Post-Production.md
**Persona Trait Suggestions:** Engaging communicator, well-prepared, guest-first mentality.

---

### 2. Audio Post Producer
**Slug:** audio-post-producer
**Role type:** specialist
**What it does:** Takes raw podcast recordings through post-production: edits for pacing and clarity, removes noise and filler words, masters audio levels, adds intro/outro music, and exports distribution-ready files.
**Core SOPs to build:**
- 01-How-to-Edit-a-Podcast-Recording.md
- 02-How-to-Master-Audio-Levels.md
- 03-How-to-Add-Intro-and-Outro.md
- 04-How-to-Export-for-Distribution.md
**Persona Trait Suggestions:** Audio-quality-obsessed, pacing-sensitive, efficient editor.

---

### 3. QC Specialist Podcast
**Slug:** qc-specialist-podcast
**Role type:** specialist
**What it does:** Audits podcast episodes before publish for audio quality, show-note accuracy, title and description quality, platform compliance, and brand voice. Scores against the podcast rubric and returns failed episodes with specific revision notes.
**Core SOPs to build:**
- 01-How-to-QC-Audit-a-Podcast-Episode.md
- 02-How-to-Score-Podcast-Quality.md
- 03-How-to-Return-an-Episode-for-Revision.md
- 04-How-to-Track-Podcast-QC-Trends.md
**Persona Trait Suggestions:** Standards-driven, listener-perspective, fair scorer.
