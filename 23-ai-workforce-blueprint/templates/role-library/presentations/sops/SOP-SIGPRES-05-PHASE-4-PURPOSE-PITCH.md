**Cluster:** Signature Presentation Doctrine (Phase 4 of 4).

## 1. THE RULE

> "A purpose pitch, not a profit pitch. Let the brand create trust so you don't hard-sell — position, don't push. People buy clarity, and so do you."

Phase 4 is the deck's long emotional crescendo: introduce the offer, prove it, create honest urgency, remove risk, and close on the transformation — never on the price. Structured by **N.E.E.I.T.** across the **4-Quadrant method**. Slides 61–100; label the phase with its name + purpose (Directive 10). This is the ONLY phase where the q7 offer name(s), price, and enroll CTAs are permitted — and they are REQUIRED here (`AF-SP-OFFER-UNDECLARED` fires if a q7 offer never appears in the pitch tokens).

---

## 2. THE FOUR QUADRANTS

- **Q1 — Setting the Stage.** Emotional connection; the title IS the transformation; aspirational + relatable. Tone: aspirational, empathetic, transformative.
- **Q2 — Unveiling the Transformation (heart of the pitch).** Introduce the offer (the exact q7 name); benefit-driven bullets; social proof and testimonials; the unique value. "People buy clarity" — spell out the offer with zero ambiguity. Tone: empowering, inspiring, persuasive.
- **Q3 — Amplifying Desire & Urgency.** Future-pace; **honest** scarcity/urgency (never fabricated — e.g. a real founding-cohort limit); overcome objections; clear CTAs. Tone: highly persuasive, emotionally charged, action-oriented.
- **Q4 — Closing with Conviction.** A powerful emotional close; risk-reversal/guarantee; an irresistible CTA; an inspiring send-off. Tone: unwavering conviction, infectious enthusiasm, bold inspiration.

---

## 3. VALUE-FIRST DOCTRINE

- **Price only matters in the absence of value** — relentlessly build value, then pricing is a non-issue. Make the **purpose-pitch-vs-profit-pitch** contrast explicit where the frame calls for it (the Rulebook does this on dedicated slides).
- **Movement + Message + Methodology = Manifestation** — Phase 4 is where the Movement invitation lands (join the movement, not just buy the thing).
- **Goal with cold audiences:** not that they learn YOUR name — that YOU capture THEIR names (list-building through value). The purpose pitch earns the next step, it doesn't extract it.
- The **signature close** is frame-specific (SOP-SIGPRES-06): a roll-call ending "…AND YOU!" (Rulebook), a blessing "My Prayer for YOU" (Vault), a poetic fill-in-the-blank manifesto (Quest — Directive 13's exemplar), or a fresh manifesto-grade close (Original).

- **Band:** slides 61–100, contiguous after Phase 3; floor ≥ 40 (`AF-SP-PHASE-RANGE`). A floor, not a fixed span — when the deck expands past 100, Phase 4 carries the tail (e.g. 61–103).
- **Bridge:** the first pitch slide sits directly after the last teaching slide (`AF-SP-BRIDGE`, checked by the no-pitch prover).
- **Label slide:** name + purpose (`AF-SP-PHASE-LABEL`).
- **N.E.E.I.T. + 4-Quadrant markers:** required in this phase (`AF-SP-QUADRANT`). Phase 4 is the third quadrant-required phase (1/2/4).
- **Suggested image / section hook / central-hook reprises:** every slide carries a `suggested_image` (`AF-SP-IMG-SUGGESTION`); the phase carries one distinct section hook and typically the central-hook reprises land here on their dedicated pure-typography slides (SOP-SIGPRES-06).
