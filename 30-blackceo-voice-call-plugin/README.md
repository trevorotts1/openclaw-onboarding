# Skill 30 - BlackCEO Voice Call Plugin

**What it does:** Gives your AI agent the ability to make and receive phone calls in your cloned voice.

**Providers used:**
- **Telnyx** - Phone call infrastructure ($0.002/min)
- **Fish Audio** - Voice generation (your cloned voice, s2-pro model)

**Start here:** Read `INSTALL.md` and follow every step in order.

**Full reference:** `SKILL.md` covers architecture, configuration, call modes, pricing, and troubleshooting.

**API docs:** `references/fish-audio-api.md` and `references/telnyx-config.md`
