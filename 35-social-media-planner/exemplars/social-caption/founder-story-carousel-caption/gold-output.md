# Exemplar — social-caption / founder-story-carousel-caption

Fictional brand: **Harbor & Pine Coaching** (illustrative only — no real
client, person, or company). Deliverable: an Instagram carousel caption for
a founder-story post (playbook.md Section 18 zone system — hook line, body,
soft CTA).

---

**Hook (line 1, before "...more"):**

I almost shut this down in year two. Here's what I'd tell that version of
me.

**Body:**

Eighteen months in, I had exactly four clients and a spreadsheet that told
me the math didn't work. Every "expert" thread said the same thing: post
more, charge more, hustle harder.

None of that was the actual problem.

The actual problem: I was building the business I thought I was supposed to
want, not the one I actually did. Different clients, different hours,
different everything — I kept optimizing a shape that was never mine to
begin with.

The pivot wasn't a rebrand. It was one honest conversation with myself
about what "success" was actually supposed to feel like day to day, not
just what it was supposed to look like on a slide.

Swipe through for the three questions that conversation started with — the
same three I still ask before saying yes to anything new.

**Soft CTA (final line):**

If you're in your own year-two spreadsheet moment right now — which
question hit? Drop it below, I read every one.
