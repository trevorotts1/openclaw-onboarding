> Solves the daily *"what do I write today?"* problem. A rotation that cycles the **6 Attractive Character storylines** across the broadcast schedule — each slot pre-wired to a value-ladder offer. This is the *programming layer* on top of the `daily-seinfeld-sequence` send engine.

## TRIGGER
Recurring editorial planning over the existing `seinfeld-broadcast` segment — one storyline slot per broadcast day. No new contact trigger.

## CHANNELS

## SEQUENCE — the 6-storyline rotation (each tied to a Value-Ladder offer)
| # | Storyline | Plot template (DCS Secret #6) | Offer tie |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Loss & Redemption** | *"I had everything… Then ___ happened. I had to figure my way out… it turned out to be a blessing… Now I ___."* | Core transformation offer |
| 2 | **Us vs Them** | Call out "talkers vs do-ers"; force a side; polarize | Community / membership / ascension |
| 3 | **Before & After** | *"First I was ___. Now I'm ___."* | The result-product |
| 4 | **Amazing Discovery** | *"Wait til you hear about this amazing new thing I just discovered!"* | Webinar / teleseminar / new offer |
| 5 | **Secret Telling** | *"I've got a secret… if you want to find out, you need to ___."* | Lead magnet / next funnel up the ladder |
| 6 | **Third-Person Testimonial** | Tell a customer's win as the story | Proof-driven / high-ticket / application |

Wrap every slot in the `seinfeld-broadcast-email` Hook → Story → Offer shell. Rotate weekly; refresh stories monthly.
