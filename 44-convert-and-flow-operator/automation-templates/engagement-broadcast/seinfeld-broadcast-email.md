## SEQUENCE — internal structure of ONE email
1. **Subject / Hook** — curiosity + open loop. Edwards headline families: `[True Story]…`, `[X] is like [unexpected Y]`, Warning, How-To-with-a-twist, "even if". About **them**, not you.
2. **Lead / pattern interrupt** — open in the middle, at the point of high drama or absurdity.
3. **Story (about nothing)** — 90% entertainment; flaws + polarity; one–two sentences per line.
4. **Pivot (Stealth / Columbo close)** — *"So, why do I tell you this?…"* → connect the lesson to the offer with *"which means…"* + ≥1 Reason People Buy.
5. **Offer + single CTA** — core offer / other product / affiliate; one UTM-tagged link.
6. **P.S.** — personality, a flaw, or an honest reason to act (book P.S.: *"no emailing me telling me it's not healthy"*).
