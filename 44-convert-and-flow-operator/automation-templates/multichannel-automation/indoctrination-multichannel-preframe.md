# Indoctrination Pre-Sell Sequence (Multichannel Story Series Before Cart Opens)

- **id:** `indoctrination-multichannel-preframe`
- **Category:** multichannel-automation
- **Aliases:** Pre-Launch Indoctrination Sequence · Sequence That Sells · 4-Video Indoctrination Series · Multichannel Pre-Sell · Warm-Up Automation · Product Launch Pre-Frame
- **Source:** Marketing Secrets Blackbook — **Secret #65: The Sequence Is What Sells** (Russell Brunson). Supporting: DotCom Secrets Secret #6 (Attractive Character), Expert Secrets (Product Launch Funnel), BRUNSON-FUNNEL-LIBRARY Entry #17.

> "A while back, I sold a List Hacking course. I could have just created an ad and sent people right into a funnel and made money. But instead, we did a series of four indoctrination videos. You can make these videos and send them out in emails, messenger bots, texts, ads...it really doesn't matter what medium you use." — Marketing Secrets Blackbook, Secret #65

> "By the time they get to the actual sales video, the series has already done the selling. They know my story, my hardships, my success, my expertise, and my values. Offering the product with a solid pitch will just be the cherry on top." — Marketing Secrets Blackbook, Secret #65

## What it is
Before the cart opens, 3–4 story episodes are delivered across email, Messenger, SMS, and retargeting ads. Each episode is a personal narrative from the Attractive Character's life — NOT a product pitch. By the time the prospect sees the offer, the relationship is built, the trust is earned, and the sale is already half-made. The cart-open email is the cherry on top.

**Key rule:** Episodes 1–4 have zero product pitch, zero buy CTAs. Only story. Only teaching. Only relationship.

## TRIGGER
- **What starts it:** Contact added to pre-launch list (tag `pre-launch-list` added) — typically by opting in to a waitlist, registering for a webinar, or being segmented from an existing list before a launch.
- **Fires:** Once per contact per campaign. Re-entry guard: tag `indoc-started` NOT present.

## CHANNELS
Email (primary) · Facebook Messenger (story mirror) · SMS (nudge on non-open) · Retargeting/Social Ads (video story creative)

## NO-PITCH RULE
Episodes 1–4 contain zero product mention, zero price reveal, zero "buy" CTA. The only CTAs in episodes 1–4 are stay-tuned CTAs: "Watch for tomorrow's email." This is the key to why the sequence works. Brunson: "Don't leave anything to chance, and remember it's the sequence that sells."

## THE SEQUENCE

### Episode 1 — Origin / Backstory (Day 1)
**Channels:** Email + Messenger mirror + Retargeting ad (video)  
**Subject:** `How I [did the embarrassing/wrong/humble thing] and what it cost me`

Brunson's Example: His List Hacking Episode 1 was about "how I started my business in my duplex and got in trouble so many times for doing things ALL wrong."

**Body framework:**
1. Open in the middle of the story (high drama first)
2. The humble origin — specific place, time, embarrassing detail
3. What went wrong and what it cost
4. One-sentence takeaway — don't solve the problem yet
5. Bridge: "Tomorrow I want to tell you about [tease Episode 2]"
6. No pitch. No product mention.

---

### Episode 2 — Obsession / Expertise Discovery (Day 2–3)
**Channels:** Email + Messenger mirror + SMS nudge (if email not opened)  
**Subject:** `The weird obsession that taught me everything I know about [result]`

Brunson's Example: His List Hacking Episode 2 was "about me and my obsession with junk mail and how it taught me so much about marketing."

**Body framework:**
1. Brief bridge from Episode 1
2. The obsession — what the AC became fascinated with and why (specific, personal)
3. What the obsession taught — the unexpected insight behind their expertise
4. A parable that illustrates the core principle of what you're about to sell
5. Bridge to Episode 3

**SMS nudge (if email not opened in 24h):** `Hey [name] — sent you [Episode 2 title] yesterday. Quick read: [link]`

---

### Episode 3 — Teaching / The Method, Light Version (Day 3–4)
**Channels:** Email + Messenger mirror + Retargeting ad  
**Subject:** `The [simple principle] that changed everything about [topic]`

Brunson's Example: His List Hacking Episode 3 was "about list building and do a bit more teaching."

**Body framework:**
1. Brief bridge from Episode 2
2. One core principle or counterintuitive truth from experience
3. Illustrated by a parable or client case study
4. Forward-looking statement: "This is the core of what I'll be sharing with you..."
5. First stay-tuned CTA: "Make sure you're watching your email — the next message is important." (Not buy — stay-tuned only)

---

### Episode 4 — Values / Mission (Day 4–5) — OPTIONAL
**Channels:** Email + Messenger mirror  
**Subject:** `Why I really do this (it's not what you think)`

Brunson's Example: His List Hacking Episode 4 was "one of me in Kenya helping build orphanages and showing that a percentage of my money goes to this mission each year."

**Body framework:**
1. The deeper mission beyond revenue
2. Specific story of the mission in action (specific person, place, impact)
3. How the offer connects to this mission
4. Bridge to cart open: "Tomorrow I'm releasing [product name] — and I want you to be first to hear about it..."

> This episode must be authentic. If the AC does not have a genuine values/mission story, skip it entirely. Fabricating a values story destroys trust.

---

### Episode 5 — Cart Open (Launch Day)
**Channels:** Email + SMS announcement + Messenger announcement  
**Subject:** `It's live — [offer name] is now available`

**Body framework:**
1. Bridge from the story series: brief recap of what they learned in Episodes 1–4
2. Offer presentation: what it is, what it does, what the transformation is
3. Value stack (if applicable) and price reveal
4. CTA: "Get access here: [offer_page_url]"
5. Deadline (if real): "Open until [date/time]"

**SMS:** `Hey [name] — [offer name] is live! [AC name] just opened the cart. Grab it here: [link]`  
**Messenger:** `Hey [name] — the [offer name] I've been building up to is live now. Here's your link: [link]`

## INDOCTRINATION PRINCIPLE (from the book)
Per Marketing Secrets Blackbook Secret #65: the indoctrination episodes "are not hard pitches. They were just stories." The prospect comes to know "my story, my hardships, my success, my expertise, and my values" BEFORE the pitch arrives. The selling happens inside the relationship building, not during the offer reveal.

The medium is flexible: "You can make these videos and send them out in emails, messenger bots, texts, ads...it really doesn't matter what medium you use." Written emails work as well as video.

## COPY PERSONA + SCRIPT
- **Architecture:** Attractive Character (DCS Secret #6) — all four AC elements across the series: Backstory (Ep1), Parables (Ep2–3), Character Flaws (the failures in Ep1), Polarity (Ep4 values/stand).
- **Email tone:** First-person, journal-entry style. Short paragraphs. Personal. Friend's message, not a newsletter.
- **SMS tone:** Ultra-short reference only. "Did you see [story title]? [link]"
- **Messenger tone:** Casual 2–3 sentence DM. Link inline.
- **Ad tone:** Video — AC talking directly to camera, telling the story. Low production value often converts better than high-polish. Authenticity is the creative.

## FLEXIBILITY — Core Principle

> This template is a **GUIDE and a RESOURCE, never a rule or requirement.** It must not dominate the user's desire.

| Mode | When it applies | What the system does |
|------|----------------|----------------------|
| **1 — Explicit desire** | User has their own pre-launch story plan | Do exactly that. Template is reference only. |
| **2 — User is unsure** | User asks how to warm up list before launch | Suggest 3–4 story episodes, explain the no-pitch rule; let user decide. |
| **3 — Just do it** | "Build the pre-launch warm-up" | Build all 4 episodes + cart-open + SMS + Messenger as described. |

Number of episodes (3 or 4), days between episodes (1–3), Episode 4 (optional) — all are recommended defaults the user can change. The template assists; it never dominates.

- **Custom values:** `{{offer_name}}`, `{{cart_open_date}}`, `{{cart_close_date}}`, `{{offer_page_url}}`, `{{ac_name}}`

**Workflow nodes:**
1. Trigger: Tag Added `pre-launch-list`. Filter: `indoc-started` NOT present.
2. Add Tag `indoc-started`
3. Email `INDOC-01 Origin/Backstory` (Day 1)
4. If/Else: `messenger-subscribed` → Messenger `INDOC-M01 Episode 1 Mirror`
5. Wait 1–2 days
6. Email `INDOC-02 Obsession/Expertise`
7. If/Else: email not opened in 24h AND `sms-subscribed` → SMS `INDOC-S01 Episode 2 Nudge`
8. If/Else: `messenger-subscribed` → Messenger `INDOC-M02 Episode 2 Mirror`
9. Wait 1–2 days
10. Email `INDOC-03 Teaching/Method`
11. If/Else: `messenger-subscribed` → Messenger `INDOC-M03`
12. Wait 1–2 days
13. (Optional) Email `INDOC-04 Values/Mission`
14. Wait 1 day
15. Add Tag `indoc-complete`
16. Email `INDOC-05 Cart Open` (or trigger from separate campaign on cart-open date)
17. If/Else: `sms-subscribed` → SMS `INDOC-S02 Cart Open Announcement`
18. If/Else: `messenger-subscribed` → Messenger `INDOC-M04 Cart Open`

**Purchase interrupt:** At any point after cart open → Add `purchased-[offer-id]`; stop remaining sales emails; start buyer onboarding.
