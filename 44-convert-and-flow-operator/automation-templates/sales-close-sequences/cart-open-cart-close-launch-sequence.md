# Cart-Open / Cart-Close Launch Sequence

**id:** `cart-open-cart-close-launch-sequence` · **category:** sales-close-sequences
**Aliases:** launch sequence, cart launch, promotional window, product launch emails, open cart close cart, Jeff Walker launch email sequence, sideways Perfect Webinar emails
**Source:** Expert Secrets (Brunson — Product Launch / Sideways Perfect Webinar, The Stack) · DotCom Secrets (Brunson — Follow-Up Funnels, 3 Closes) · Marketing Secrets Blackbook (Brunson — Secret 51: Urgency & Scarcity) · The Funnel Hacker's Cookbook (Product Launch Funnel recipe) · Copywriting Secrets (Edwards — 13-part offer block, FOMO future-pacing, guarantee)

> **The full promotional arc of a time-limited offer window.** Three phases: PRE-CART (anticipation, desire-building before the cart opens), CART-OPEN + DURING-CART (announcement, Stack, proof, logic close), and CART-CLOSING (urgency cadence driven by Marketing Secrets Blackbook Secret 51: limited time + limited pricing + limited quantity stacked together).
>
> Brunson calls the content version of this the **"Sideways Perfect Webinar"** — 3 pre-launch videos playing the same belief-breaking role as the 3 secrets in the Perfect Webinar, followed by the cart-open as the close. This template covers both the email version (no launch videos, pure email) and the launch-video version.

---

## FLEXIBILITY PRINCIPLE

This template is a **guide and a resource, never a rule or a requirement.** Three operating modes:

| Mode | What the system does |
|------|---------------------|
| **User has an explicit desire** | Build exactly what the user specifies. This template is optional reference only — never impose it or override their choice. |
| **User is unsure** | Present this proven sequence and explain why each phase works. Let the user decide what to adopt, skip, or modify. |
| **User wants it handled ("just do it")** | Execute the full sequence from this template, adapting copy to the user's offer and deadline. |

Always overridable, mixable, customizable, and ignorable.

## TRIGGER
- Cart-open launch date reached (calendar-based).
- Webinar/event concludes and cart opens immediately after.
- Product launch video 3 is published and cart opens.
- Operator manually adds the `launch-list-[offer]` tag.

**Entry condition:** Contact is warm (has been through SOS or other indoctrination); not already tagged `buyer-[offer]`.
**Exit:** Purchase → buyer onboarding + value-ladder ascension, STOP all launch emails. Cart closes for non-buyers → Seinfeld Daily Sequence (or optional downsell first).

## CHANNELS
Email (all phases) · SMS (cart-open + T-24h, T-1h urgency) · Facebook/Instagram Message (cart-open + cart-close beats) · Desktop push (cart-open + T-24h) · Retargeting ads (sync warm audience during cart window) · Sales/checkout page with live countdown timer (synchronized to cart-close datetime custom field)

### PHASE 1 — PRE-CART (days -5 to -1)

| Step | Day | Beat | Subject angle | Body framework | Persona |
|------|-----|------|---------------|----------------|---------|
| 1 | -5 to -3 | **Anticipation** — tease the upcoming opportunity; tell subscribers something is coming and WHY they should care. Hook/Story (Edwards) leading to 'watch for my email on [date].' No link yet. | "I'm opening something special on [date]…" / "Something I almost didn't do" | Hook (brief story making the promise feel real) → what's coming → the open date → P.S. with date again | Brunson Attractive Character + Edwards curiosity/open-loop |

| Step | Timing | Beat | Subject | Framework | Persona |
|------|--------|------|---------|-----------|---------|

| 7 | T-24h | **24-hour warning** — fast-action bonus expires + cost-of-inaction future-pacing (Edwards: 'picture yourself in 6 months still stuck in [pain] because today you did nothing'). | "24 hours — [bonus] disappears tonight" | Deadline sentence → bonus expiry → future-pace (6-month before/after) → 1-sentence offer + price → CTA. ## COPY PERSONA & SCRIPT
- **Primary:** Brunson — Product Launch / Sideways Perfect Webinar / **The Stack** / **Secret 51** (limited time/price/quantity — all three stacked).
- **Supporting:** Edwards — the 13-part offer block (headline → shocking statement → problem → agitate → solution → bullets → credibility → proof → offer+price → bonuses → guarantee → CTA → P.S.); FOMO future-pacing ("picture yourself 6 months from now if you do nothing"); single CTA rule; P.S. deadline restatement.
- **Scarcity levers (Blackbook Secret 51 verbatim):** "There are two words that increase every sale you make: urgency and scarcity… Limited pricing / Limited quantities / Limited time. The best offers use all three at once."

## GHL BUILD (Skill 44 → GoHighLevel)
**Workflow:** `Cart-Open / Cart-Close Launch — [Offer Name]`

1. **Trigger:** `Tag Added = launch-list-[offer]`. Filter: `buyer-[offer]` must be absent.
2. **PRE-CART:** `Wait Until` [D-5 date] → send anticipation email → `Wait Until` [D-1 date] → send day-before email + SMS.
3. **CART-OPEN:** `Wait Until` [cart-open datetime] → simultaneous Email + SMS + FB/IG message action + desktop push. Add tag `saw-cart-open`.
4. **DURING-CART:** `Wait Until` [D+2] → logic/proof email → `Wait Until` [D+4] → guarantee email.
5. **CART-CLOSE URGENCY:** All five beats (T-48h, T-24h, T-3h, T-1h, close) use `Wait Until` calculated from the single `cart-close-datetime` custom field — this is the same field the on-page countdown timer reads from.
6. **POST-CLOSE:** `If/Else` on tag `buyer-[offer]` → buyers: buyer onboarding workflow; non-buyers: optional downsell email, then enroll `seinfeld-daily-sequence`.

**Critical GHL notes:**
- **`cart-close-datetime` custom field is the single source of truth** for all T-minus waits AND the countdown timer on the sales/order page. Set it before the workflow starts.
- **Purchase Goal** (`buyer-[offer]`) at any step → immediately exit, enroll buyer onboarding, STOP all launch emails.
- **Abandoned cart branch:** if `checkout-started` tag exists without `buyer-[offer]` → fire `abandoned-cart-recovery` workflow immediately (runs alongside or replaces the urgency schedule).
- Sync a retargeting ad audience for contacts tagged `saw-cart-open` who do not have `buyer-[offer]` during the window.

## INTEGRITY GUARDRAILS
- Cart-close datetime is real — the offer/price genuinely changes after it. Never extend a deadline without announcing a new one in advance (Blackbook S51; Edwards integrity rule).
- All three scarcity levers must be true as stated (50 spots = enforce 50 spots; price rises = raise the price; bonus disappears = remove it).
- Stack values are defensible — real standalone value, not inflated fiction (Edwards "sell dollars for dimes" done honestly).
- Proof/testimonials real and representative of typical results.
