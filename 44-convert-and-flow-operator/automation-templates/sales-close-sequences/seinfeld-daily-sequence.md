# Daily Seinfeld Sequence — Entertain-Then-Sell Engagement

**id:** `seinfeld-daily-sequence` · **category:** sales-close-sequences
**Aliases:** Seinfeld emails, daily email show, entertain and sell, ongoing nurture, Soap-Opera follow-on
**Source:** DotCom Secrets (Brunson — Seinfeld Sequence + Attractive Character) · Copywriting Secrets (Edwards — Hook/Story/Offer) · Marketing Secrets Blackbook (publish daily; Stack Don't Switch)

> **Role:** The maintenance layer that runs *after* the Soap Opera Sequence. It keeps the list warm and rotates **Emotion / Logic / Fear** closes episode-by-episode while ascending the value ladder. The "show about nothing" that always pivots to an offer.

---

## TRIGGER
- Contact tagged `sos-complete`, **or** any engaged subscriber on the main list.
- **Exit:** unsubscribe / hard bounce / sustained disengagement → re-engagement then sunset. Otherwise it runs indefinitely.

## CHANNELS
Email (daily / near-daily broadcast) · optional SMS for high-urgency episodes · optional social mirror.

## CADENCE
Daily or near-daily, ongoing. New graduates get an **evergreen drip**; everyone engaged gets **live broadcasts**.

---

## EMAIL ANATOMY — Hook / Story / Offer (Edwards)
Each email is **~90% entertaining episode**, then segues to one offer:
1. **HOOK** — curiosity subject + first line that earns the open (8 of 10 only read the subject).
2. **STORY** — a short, entertaining "about nothing" episode carrying ONE lesson.
3. **SEGUE** — one sentence pivoting the lesson to the offer.
4. **OFFER** — ONE clear CTA to a value-ladder product/funnel.

---

## THE SEQUENCE — rotating episode types

| # | Episode type | Beat | Subject angle | Close flavor |
|---|--------------|------|---------------|--------------|
| 1 | **Loss & Redemption** | Setback → comeback; lesson = the principle behind the offer | "The week I lost everything (and what saved me)" | Emotion |
| 2 | **Us vs. Them (Polarity)** | Old broken way vs. new opportunity; repel wrong people, attract the tribe | "Why I'll never do [old way] again" | Emotion / belief |
| 3 | **Amazing Discovery** | An experiment/result, curiosity-led | "I tested this for 30 days. The result shocked me." | Logic |
| 4 | **Secret Telling** | Behind-the-scenes / counter-intuitive truth | "The thing the gurus won't tell you about [topic]" | Logic |
| 5 | **Third-Person Testimonial** | Customer story does the selling | "How Sarah went from [before] to [after] in 6 weeks" | Logic / proof |
| 6 | **Cliffhanger Arc (2–3 day)** | Open a loop today, pay it off tomorrow; time-bound offer rides the resolution | Day 1 "I almost didn't hit send…" → Day 2 "here's what happened" | Fear / urgency |

---

## COPY PERSONA & SCRIPT
- **Primary:** Brunson — **Attractive Character** (Backstory, Parables, Character Flaws, Polarity) + storyline types (Loss & Redemption, Us vs. Them, Before/During/After, Amazing Discovery, Secret Telling, Third-Person Testimonial).
- **Supporting:** Edwards — **Hook / Story / Offer**, curiosity hooks ("unlikely character + timing + result"), exactly ONE CTA. Go negative on the **method**, never a named competitor.
- **Stack Don't Switch (Blackbook Secret 43):** keep ONE new opportunity; every email's offer *stacks onto* it — don't introduce a brand-new opportunity each day.

## GHL BUILD (Skill 44 → GoHighLevel)
**Two-mode build:**
- **(A) Evergreen Workflow** `Seinfeld — Daily Entertain-Then-Sell (Ongoing)` — trigger `Tag Added = sos-complete`; drips the rotating Hook/Story/Offer bank, Wait 1 day (send window 8am tz) between episodes.
- **(B) Live broadcasts** — send daily to an **"Engaged 30-day" smart list** (opened ≥1 email in 30 days) via GHL Email Broadcast.

**If/Else & Goals:**
- Offer link clicked 2+ times, no purchase → enroll in that offer's `three-closes-master-followup` or `scarcity-deadline-close`.
- No open 30–45 days → re-engagement workflow → sunset (protects deliverability / sender reputation).
- Purchase of offer X → suppress X emails, ascend to next value-ladder offer.

**Notes:** Maintain the engaged smart list for broadcasts; evergreen workflow feeds new graduates. Publish daily. One CTA per email.

## INTEGRITY GUARDRAILS
Stories true; testimonials real and provable; negative aimed at the old method only; mail only the engaged.

## KPIs
Daily open rate · CTR to offers · revenue per subscriber/month · RFMS recency · value-ladder ascension rate.
