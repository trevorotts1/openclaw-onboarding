## 0. What the funnel is

A request-driven pipeline that produces the signature funnel end-to-end: **12-section Hero copy →
per-section long-form image prompts (5,000–19,000 chars) → Kie.ai `gpt-image-2` images in the
signature look → GHL media folder + uploads → HTML assembly → GHL funnel build with 3/5/7
configurable steps and accept/decline branching → preview + human approval → optional 10 promo
emails.** The funnel spans up to seven steps: **Main → (Checkout, 7-step only) → Upsell-1 →
Downsell-1 → Upsell-2 → Downsell-2 → Thank-You.**

Two things are **SACRED and machine-enforced** (never advisory):
1. The **12 section names** and every **copy character/word band** below.
2. The **image-prompt character band: 5,000–19,000 chars** per prompt.

Both are enforced by fail-closed provers (deterministic measurer, stripped-length, AF-FUN-* autofails
— never agent self-score).

## 1. THE 12-SECTION COPY SYSTEM (SACRED — verbatim bands)

Label each section by name in the output. Respect the counts exactly ("You are forbidden from
exceeding my word count for each section"). Each section makes a compelling argument to act and stays
in harmony with the one before it (the Harmony Chain).

| Sec | Name (verbatim) | Hard band | Structural rules |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **The Big Bold Claim / The Big Bold Promise** | 180–225 chars | The product title MUST appear. Labeled CTA. The single most important section; the first thing the reader sees. ONE big bold promise. |
| 2 | **The Big Bold Pain 1** | 180–225 chars | 2nd person. NO question format. Labeled CTA. Circumstantial pain (what their days look like). |
| 3 | **The Big Bold Pain 2** | 180–225 chars | 2nd person. NO question format. Labeled CTA. Private pain (what they say to themselves). |
| 4 | **The Big Bold Pain 3** | 180–225 chars | 2nd person. NO question format. Labeled CTA. Witnessed pain (who is watching). |
| 5 | **The Big Bold Why** | ≤30 words | Starts "That's the reason why…". Ends with a motivational CTA. |
| 6 | **The Big Bold Who** | ≤30 words | 3–6 personas, each with what they seek. **NO CTA** in this section. |
| 7 | **The Big Bold What** | 70–120 words | 5–10 specific bullets, each naming exactly what the buyer gets. |
| 8 | **The Big Bold Benefit 1** | ≤30 words | Felt benefit (this week). **NO CTA button.** |
| 9 | **The Big Bold Benefit 2** | ≤30 words | Measured benefit (this quarter). **NO CTA button.** |
| 10 | **The Big Bold Benefit 3** | ≤30 words | Become benefit (this life). Carries the **inspirational CTA button** (peak-end). |
| 11 | **The Big How To** | 100–150 words; **NO CTA button** | 5–10 steps (ideal 7). Steps 1–6 each **89–116 chars**; step 7 **≤170 chars**. MUST include a share-with-a-friend step, a check-email-for-a-bonus step, a founder-personal-text step, and a join-the-community step. The final step is the in-copy motivational CTA. |
| 12 | **The Big Bold Heartfelt Message** | 100–150 words | **6 labeled parts.** Part 2 (**The Big Struggle**) starts "I used to be just like you…". Parts: The Big Bold Heartfelt Message / The Big Struggle / The Big Decision / The Big Reason / The Big Invite / The Big Passionate Close (signed by the founder). |

**Additional hard constraints (verbatim):** Sections 1–4 each ≥180 and ≤225 characters. Section 6 has
a minimum of 3 and a maximum of 6 personas and NO CTA. Section 7 is 70–120 words with 5–10 bullets.
Section 11 step band: minimum 89 chars, forbidden below 83, forbidden above 116; step 7 forbidden to
exceed 170. Section 12 is exactly six labeled parts.

### Generation craft (governs the writer; the bands above are the machine bar)
- **Harmony Chain** — the 12 sections are ONE escalating argument; carry one word/image/idea from
  section N−1 into section N; the reader never feels a topic reset.
- **One CTA Voice** — choose ONE first-person, possession/transformation CTA phrase per page
  (Start My ___, Claim My ___, Take It Back, Unlock My ___, Reserve My Seat); label it `CTA: <phrase>`
  in every CTA-bearing section. Section 6 carries no CTA; Section 11 uses no button (step 7 is the CTA).
- **Pain Ladder** — Sec 2 circumstantial, Sec 3 private, Sec 4 witnessed; three DIFFERENT pains, never
  the same pain reworded; each in 2nd person, present tense, never a question.
- **Benefit Ladder** — Sec 8 felt, Sec 9 measured, Sec 10 become; rising altitude, peak-end CTA on Sec 10.
- **Specificity Laws** — numbers beat adjectives; moments beat categories; **truth gate** — every
  scarcity claim, bonus, founder text, and community must be confirmed real at intake (Q16). The
  system never fabricates urgency.

## 2. DERIVED PAGE PROFILES

### Upsell (OTO1)
Same 12-section format BUT **exclude Sections 8–11** (benefits + how-to) and **replace Section 12**
(founder letter) with **"7 Reasons To Commit To Your ____ Future"** (fill the blank with an
identity-charged, on-brand phrase), **renumbered to Section 8**: exactly **7** numbered reasons,
escalating practical → identity, reason 7 lands the CTA. A CTA in every section; true scarcity + FOMO
only. Momentum frame on Section 1 (confirm the win, then extend it — never restart the sale).

### Downsell (OTO1-decline)
Same exclusions; **replace Section 12** with **"When Time Runs Out"** — the **7 things they'll miss**
(scarcity/urgency/one-time value), **renumbered to Section 8**. CTA every section. Graceful-concession
frame on Section 1 (honor the no, then reduce the barrier: smaller/lighter/staged offer).

### Upsell 2 (OTO2)
Same sacred derivation; Section 8 = **"7 Reasons To Commit To Your ____ Future"** with a blank-fill that
MUST differ from OTO1. **Categorically different offer** (change KIND, not size). Journey-neutral
Section 1 anchored on the ORIGINAL purchase. The final-door frame ("this is the last page where this
exists").

### Downsell 2 (OTO2-decline)
Section 8 = **"When Time Runs Out"** (final-door misses). The dignity close (they've said no twice):
the smallest true yes. **After Downsell 2 the funnel NEVER pitches again** — the Thank-You page is clean.

### Thank-You (every funnel ends here)
Three labeled parts (NOT the 12-section hero, NOT a sales page):
- **TY-1 — "The Big Bold Welcome":** 120–180 chars. Celebrate the decision + name the product title.
  Second person, celebratory, zero selling.
- **TY-2 — "What Happens Next":** 4–6 numbered steps, each **89–116 chars** (Section 11 discipline),
  momentum-ordered: email bonus / founder text / community / share-a-friend (mandatory slots first).
- **TY-3 — "The Big Empowering Close":** ≤170 chars. Destiny register, signed by the founder.
- **NO offer CTAs anywhere**; utility buttons only (Join The Community / Share With A Friend /
  Add To Calendar). One image maximum. Truth gate at full strength.

### Checkout (7-step only)
A dedicated signature-styled order page with minimal microcopy (the cleanest home for GHL's order form,
which one-click upsells require). No 12-section gate.

## 3. THE 3 / 5 / 7 FUNNEL MATRIX (configurable at intake, Q10)

| GHL step | Page type | 3-step | 5-step | 7-step |
|---|---|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| 1 | **Main** — 12-section hero (order capture inline for 3/5-step) | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| 2 | **Checkout** — dedicated order page (7-step only) | — | — | ✓ |
| next | **Upsell 1 (OTO1)** | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| next | **Downsell 1** | — | ✓ | ✓ |
| next | **Upsell 2 (OTO2)** | — | ✓ | ✓ |
| next | **Downsell 2** | — | — | ✓ |
| last | **Thank-You** | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |

- **3-step** = Main → Upsell 1 → Thank-You.
- **5-step** = Main → Upsell 1 → Downsell 1 → Upsell 2 → Thank-You.
- **7-step** = Main → Checkout → Upsell 1 → Downsell 1 → Upsell 2 → Downsell 2 → Thank-You.

**Branching (GHL one-click upsell):** payment captured once at the order form; each downsell sits
immediately after its upsell; an upsell's **decline** falls through to its downsell, its **accept**
jumps past it; **every path terminates at Thank-You** — nobody ends a purchase on an offer page.

## 4. IMAGE SYSTEM (SACRED band: 5,000–19,000 chars per prompt)

- **Model:** Kie.ai `gpt-image-2`, **text-to-image by default** via the Skill 47 adapter. 16:9 / 2K
  defaults; Sec 4 → 16:9 (was 21:9); Sec 12 → 3:4 (was 2:3). All Midjourney syntax retired.
- **Prompt band: 5,000–19,000 characters** (stripped length; whitespace never counts), enforced by
  `prove_sf_prompt_floor.py` (two-floor gate — length floor + structure/excellence floor). A failing
  prompt physically cannot reach a paid Kie call.
- **8-block build order (every prompt):** 1 Subject & Wardrobe · 2 Composition & Shot · 3 Typography
  (text-bearing sections only; dominant for Sec 11) · 4 **Signature Grade Block** (verbatim) · 5
  Lighting · 6 Quality & Render · 7 Facial Intelligence · 8 Brand-Style + Negative Block (final
  paragraph). Front-load subject/emotion/composition; end-load the negative block. **No em dashes.**
- **Sec 11 is typography-as-art** (gallery canvases, three spelling-locked words in quotes); all other
  sections photographic; no-text sections state "no text anywhere" explicitly.
- **Provenance:** a real Kie taskId is required for every generated image (no native/placeholder), and
  every `<img>` must resolve to the GHL media host.
- **Optional `style_card_id` (FIX-XC-02c)** — when the intake supplies a registered Skill 45 `FN-` style
  card (Q18), DIU Workflow B resolves it and its **LONG tier** is embedded VERBATIM as the Brand-Style
  portion of **block 8** in every prompt (block 4's Signature Grade Block is unchanged). Unset = the
  default block 8 / brand-color Signature look — purely additive.
- **`reference_images` hook** — one optional slot, `mode: none | signature_set | client_uploads |
  signature_set+client_uploads`, **default `none`** (launch state = pure text-to-image; look carried
  entirely by the Signature Grade Block). When populated, resolved URLs pass to the adapter's
  `image_input` (≤8 refs; auto image-to-image) with a mandatory style-only guard; references logged in
  the certificate.

### THE SIGNATURE GRADE BLOCK (canonical constant — embedded verbatim in block 4 of every prompt)

> Render this image in the Trevor Otts Signature aesthetic, and treat this grading direction as the
> single most important instruction in this prompt. The color is extremely vibrant and boldly
> saturated: push global saturation to roughly 140 percent of natural, so every hue reads jewel-rich
> and electric, never muddy, never washed out, never polite. The image is heavily and deliberately
> color-graded like a high-fashion editorial cover: a distinct cinematic grade with deep, crushed,
> inky shadows set against luminous, glowing highlights, and punchy complementary color relationships
> that make the frame vibrate with energy. Contrast is high and confident. The palette is anchored to
> the brand colors supplied for this page, pushed to their richest, most luminous expression. The
> overall effect is a fashion show rendered as gallery art: disruptive, edgy, provocative, emotionally
> charged, composed like a single standalone piece of art that could be framed and hung, so striking
> that a scrolling thumb stops involuntarily. Every human subject is lit and graded with melanin-true
> intelligence: deep skin tones rendered rich, dimensional, and radiant, with warm undertones
> preserved, never ashy, never grey, never flattened. This is not natural documentary color. This is
> signature color: vivid, graded, unforgettable.

(~1,290 characters. A single constant so the look is bit-identical across all sections and pages.)

shot (16:9) · Sec 5 the rise · Sec 6 the people wall (honor representation exactly) · Sec 7 clarity

## 8. Deliverable labeling grammar (SACRED — reciprocal with Skill 56)

Every funnel deliverable — each copy doc, image prompt, generated PNG, HTML fragment, and preview — is
labeled with the fixed grammar:

```
<client>__<funnel>__<stage>__<type>__vNN
```

- `<client>` — the client/brand slug (lowercase, hyphenless; the operator supplies it — NEVER a real
  client name in this fleet-wide repo, only at runtime on the client box).
- `<funnel>` — the funnel slug (from the offer title).
- `<stage>` — one of `main` · `checkout` · `upsell1` · `downsell1` · `upsell2` · `downsell2` ·
  `thankyou` (matches the 3/5/7 matrix in §3).
- `<type>` — one of `copy` · `prompt` · `image` · `html` · `preview`.
- `vNN` — a zero-padded version (`v01`, `v02`, …); every re-author/regenerate increments it.

Double-underscore (`__`) is the field separator so single tokens may contain single hyphens. Example:
`acme__reclaim-your-mornings__upsell1__prompt__v03`.
