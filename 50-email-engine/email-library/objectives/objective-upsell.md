## Style / Definition
The Upsell objective moves an **existing customer** to a higher tier, add-on, or next-level offer using five triggers: **commitment/consistency, perceived-value multiplication, status/aspiration, fear-of-suboptimal-outcome, and convenience/efficiency.** Governing rule: **always acknowledge the previous purchase positively** — the upsell continues a good decision; it never corrects a bad one.

## Purpose & Intention
The person most likely to buy from you is the person who just did. Commitment/consistency makes ascension natural: having decided "I'm someone who invests in this," the customer *wants* subsequent decisions to match. The craft line to walk: an upsell that implies the first purchase was insufficient breeds regret and refunds; one that *celebrates* the first purchase and extends it breeds loyalty. This is also the native objective of the 12 persona styles (high-ticket / Upsell-2 emails), where voice-driven emotional range does the heavy lifting.

## How To Write It (trigger craft)
1. **Open by honoring the purchase.** Congratulate genuinely, reference what they bought, ideally note something they've already gained. Never "but," always "and."
2. **Ride commitment/consistency.** Frame the upgrade as the same identity, continued: "You've already proven you're the kind of operator who invests in systems. This is that same decision, one level up."
3. **Multiply perceived value.** Show how the upgrade makes the ORIGINAL purchase worth more: "the templates you own become 10x more powerful with the automation layer." The upgrade compounds what they have.
4. **Aspiration, not inadequacy (status).** Point at who they're becoming, never at what they lack. "Ready for the advanced room" converts; "the basic plan is limiting you" insults.
5. **Fear-of-suboptimal — use surgically.** "You'll get results either way; this path gets them in half the time." The base purchase always remains a good decision.
6. **Convenience/efficiency as closer.** Their info is on file, setup is instant, one click ascends. Make the yes frictionless.
7. **Time it to a win.** Upsell after a success moment (completed module, first result, milestone) — never during onboarding confusion or a support issue.

### Per-Buyer-Type Tactics (Upsell)
- **Spontaneous:** one-click instant unlock; "takes 10 seconds since you're already set up." Trigger emphasis: **convenience/efficiency.**
- **Methodical:** ROI math on the upgrade; side-by-side tier comparison; how it integrates with what they own. Trigger emphasis: **perceived-value multiplication.**
- **Humanistic:** deeper access to the people — smaller group, closer mentorship, inner community. Trigger emphasis: **status/aspiration (belonging-coded).**
- **Competitive:** what top performers use; performance edge of the higher tier; capped advanced room. Trigger emphasis: **status + fear-of-suboptimal.**

## Use Cases
- Post-purchase ascension sequences (tier 1 → tier 2)
- High-ticket / Upsell-2 emails written in the 12 persona styles
- Add-on and companion-product offers after a win moment
- Renewal upgrades (annual, premium support, done-with-you)
- Milestone-triggered offers ("you finished Module 3 — here's the advanced path")

## Detailed Example
**Framework:** Features-to-Benefit · **Objective:** Upsell (dominant trigger: perceived-value multiplication)

**Subject Line A:** {{contact.first_name}}, your Toolkit just earned an upgrade path
**Subject Line B:** Make what you already own work 10x harder, {{contact.first_name}}

**Preview Text:** You made a great call. Here's how to compound it.

**Email Body:**

{{contact.first_name}},

First — good call on the Toolkit. Members typically recover their first hours within two weeks, and you're set up to do exactly that.

Here's what most members discover next: **the Toolkit has a second gear.**

**The Automation Layer** means every template you already own runs on a schedule instead of a to-do list, which allows the system to produce while you're in delivery mode — or asleep.

**The Advanced Playbook Library** means the exact sequences behind our top members' results plug straight into your existing setup, which allows you to skip the trial-and-error they paid for.

Notice the theme: **this doesn't replace what you bought. It multiplies it.** Same tools, ten times the output.

Because you're already a member, your account upgrades in one click — everything you've set up carries over instantly:

**[Add the Automation Layer to my Toolkit →]**

Either way, the Toolkit will serve you well. This is simply the difference between owning the engine and turbocharging it.

[Founder Name]

## Quality Checklist
- [ ] Previous purchase acknowledged positively in the opening (mandatory)
- [ ] Upgrade framed as identity-consistent continuation, not correction
- [ ] Shows how the upgrade multiplies the ORIGINAL purchase's value
- [ ] Aspiration language only — zero inadequacy framing
- [ ] Base purchase explicitly remains a good decision
- [ ] Friction removed (one-click, carries over, instant)
- [ ] Timed to a customer win moment, not a friction moment
- [ ] Tactics tuned to buyer type (see table above)
- [ ] Standard formatting; two A/B subject lines; preview <100 chars
- [ ] Founder's actual name in the signature
