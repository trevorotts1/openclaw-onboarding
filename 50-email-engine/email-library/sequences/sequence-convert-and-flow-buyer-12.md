## Style / Definition
The **Convert & Flow (GoHighLevel) master 12-email sequence**, mapped to the four buyer types. Each of the twelve emails is assigned a fixed framework by its slot, and each slot serves the buyer band it was designed to move. It is the CRM-side workhorse of the whole system: one sequence that speaks to spontaneous, methodical, humanistic and competitive readers in turn, in the order those temperaments are most receptive.

## Purpose & Intention
A segmented list is not one audience — it is four. Spontaneous buyers decide on feel and want the point fast; methodical buyers want evidence, structure and specifics; humanistic buyers move on story and belonging; competitive buyers move on edge, status and proof. This sequence front-loads the two fast-moving spontaneous emails, works through the four methodical evidence emails, warms through the three humanistic story emails, then closes with the three competitive high-velocity emails. The single most important rule: **every email must stay in harmony with all previous emails (except #1)** — one voice, one offer, one escalating argument, twelve angles.

## How To Write It (sequence rules - all 12 emails)
1. **Framework by slot is fixed** — E1 is always the 3-B Plan, E12 is always Before-After-Bridge, and so on down the map. The framework is the slot's job; never swap them.
2. **Buyer band by slot is fixed** — E1-2 spontaneous, E3-6 methodical, E7-9 humanistic, E10-12 competitive. Write each email to the reader that slot serves.
3. **Word count: 150-300 words** per email, EXCEPT E1 (the 3-B Plan), which is deliberately shorter (<150).
4. **2 subject lines per email** (A/B split test), labeled. Most compelling info in the first 40 characters; include `{{contact.first_name}}` in the first 40 characters; 8-12 words; no pricing.
5. **ONE preview line per email** (the Convert & Flow master uses a single preview text under "Email Preview Text:"; the High-Ticket-Appointment variant is the one that uses two). Tease the core value; do not repeat the subject; under 100 characters.
6. **Harmony / continuity:** every email harmonizes with all previous emails (except #1). No email may contradict an earlier promise, reset the relationship, or re-introduce what a prior email already established.
7. **One objective per email** — the whole sequence targets a single objective (promotional / abandoned-cart / upsell / downsell) with per-buyer-type tactical variants.
8. **Founder's ACTUAL name in every closing signature** — no placeholder ships.

## Use Cases
- The default nurture / promotion sequence for a buyer-type-segmented GoHighLevel list
- Promotional launches to a warm list (one objective, twelve angles)
- Upsell and downsell campaigns to existing customers, mapped by temperament
- Abandoned-cart recovery where the list is segmented by buyer psychology
