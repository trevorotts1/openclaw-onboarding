## Style / Definition
A **12-email system built to book high-value 1:1 consult appointments** by framing the call as a rare, transformative **"strategic assessment"** — never a sales call. It runs the full buyer-type architecture (Spontaneous → Methodical → Humanistic → Competitive), a dedicated trigger set, a mandatory disruptive element per email, and its own stricter subject/preview specification. (The corpus reference model — a "strategy session" offer — is illustrative only; the system is offer-agnostic.)

## Purpose & Intention
High-ticket appointments die when the email frame says "let us sell you." This system inverts the frame: the appointment itself carries standalone value — a diagnosis the prospect cannot perform alone. Every mechanism serves that inversion: **Diagnostic Positioning** makes the call an assessment, **Exclusive Access** makes it scarce because expert time genuinely is, and **Risk-Reversal Amplification** makes attending safer than not attending. Across 12 emails the sequence escalates from establishing expertise (early), to deepening the prospect's felt gap (middle), to contrasting clarity-vs-uncertainty (late) — so by E12 the unbooked call feels like the risky option.

## How To Write It
Build each email in this order: **(1)** locate the email number on the buyer-type map below and use its assigned framework; **(2)** frame the appointment as a strategic assessment — never a sales call; **(3)** deploy the trigger(s) matching the email's arc position; **(4)** install at least one disruptive element; **(5)** write subjects and previews to the stricter specs below, choosing an unused conversational style; **(6)** verify continuity with every prior email; **(7)** run the final checklist. The subsections that follow are the complete specification.

### The 5 Psychological Triggers
1. **Exclusive Access** — the expert's calendar is genuinely finite; sessions are allocated, not advertised.
2. **Diagnostic Positioning** — the call is an assessment that produces insight, whatever the prospect decides afterward.
3. **Future-Self Manifestation** — the session as the meeting point between who they are and who they're becoming.
4. **Opportunity-Cost Illumination** — make visible what staying undiagnosed costs per month.
5. **Risk-Reversal Amplification** — stack the risk onto not attending; the session itself is the safe move.

### The 8 Disruptive Elements (≥1 per email)
**Consultation Value Inversion** (the free call is worth more than most paid products) · **Strategic Clarity Paradox** (you can't read the label from inside the jar) · **Expertise Proximity Effect** (proximity to mastery compresses timelines) · **Insight Timing** (the same insight is worth 10x at the right moment) · **Commitment Psychology Reversal** (we should be hesitant to offer, not you to accept) · **Decision Velocity** (speed of deciding predicts trajectory) · **Hidden Barrier Revelation** (the real obstacle is never the one on the surface) · **Transformation Catalyst** (one conversation as inflection point).

### Subject-Line Specification (stricter than standard)
- **TWO per email (A/B).** Length **80–87 characters**; most compelling content in the **first 55**.
- Include `{{contact.first_name}}` — **vary its position** across the sequence.
- **Exactly ONE purposeful emoji** per subject line — strategic (🎯 📊 🔍 ⚡ 🗝), opportunity (🚪 ⏳ 💎), or emotional (🔥 ✨ 💪) register, matched to the email's energy.
- Choose ONE of the **30 conversational styles** below per email — **never repeat a style within a campaign, never name the style in the email.**

### Preview-Text Specification
**TWO per email (A/B)**, each **<100 characters**, teasing exclusive strategic insight. Never repeat the subject line; never manufacture false urgency.

### Sequence Continuity (the 12-email arc)
- **Progressive strategic-value perception:** early (E1–E4) = establish expertise; middle (E5–E9) = deepen the felt gap; late (E10–E12) = contrast clarity-vs-uncertainty.
- **Callbacks:** reference prior emails naturally ("the bottleneck I named Tuesday…").
- **Incremental revelation:** each email reveals one new layer of the diagnostic story — never the whole case at once.
- **Emotional-intensity escalation:** measured → invested → urgent across the arc.
- **Objection-handling sequence:** each email quietly retires one booking objection (time, "sales call" fear, readiness, worthiness, price-of-followup…).
- **Social-proof escalation:** mentions → specifics → named outcomes as the sequence matures.

- [ ] Buyer-type alignment for the slot (E1–2 S / E3–6 M / E7–9 H / E10–12 C)
- [ ] Call framed as strategic assessment — never a sales call
- [ ] Subject lines: 2 per email, 80–87 chars, hook in first 55, `{{contact.first_name}}` position varied, exactly ONE purposeful emoji
- [ ] Subject style chosen from the 30, never repeated in-campaign, never named
- [ ] Preview text: 2 per email, <100 chars, no subject repeat, no false urgency
- [ ] ≥1 of the 8 disruptive elements present
- [ ] Correct triggers deployed (of the 5) for the email's arc position
- [ ] Continuity: harmony with all prior emails, callbacks, incremental revelation
- [ ] Formatting standard: ≤3-sentence paragraphs, bolds, CTA on own line
