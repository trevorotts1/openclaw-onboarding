# Frame — The Original

> The from-scratch door. No borrowed format signature — the client's OWN methodology becomes the
> teaching spine, and the devices/refrains/close are designed fresh in the Hook Lab and Brainstorming
> Buddy. Every frame runs the identical 4-phase skeleton; The Original still lands every sacred rule.
> The non-negotiables (slide ranges, ≥100 floor, ≤2 case studies, 3–7 steps, suggested image per
> slide, N.E.E.I.T./4-Quadrant, Phase-3-no-pitch, central + section hooks, and a manifesto-grade
> close) are enforced by the provers, not by this template.

- **signature_frame id:** `original`
- **Teaching unit:** the client's own methodology chunked into **3–7 steps** (Directive 11 expansion applies)
- **Per-step shape:** designed fresh, but each step MUST mix the Phase-3 learning elements: powerful questions, affirmations, action steps, stories, media moments, quotes, one-liners, definitions, formulas, downloadables
- **Signature devices:** designed fresh (a bespoke motif, refrain, or device battery) — but there MUST be a central hook + one section hook per phase
- **Central hook:** the one repeated chorus line; **section hooks:** one per phase

## Phase 1 — Avatar Section (slides 1–11)
- N.E.E.I.T. + 4 Quadrants. Open inside the audience's current-state story; build problem avatars then the aspirational avatar. Section-hook slide for Phase 1.

## Phase 2 — Signature Story (slides 12–24)
- N.E.E.I.T. + 4 Quadrants. Relatability → vulnerability → pain-into-purpose → legacy. End on a gripping "why". Section-hook slide for Phase 2.

## Phase 3 — Transformational Teaching (slides 25–60) — NO PITCHING
- The teaching is the client's methodology as **3–7 named steps** (chapter-style titles).
- Each step mixes the learning elements above; design a fresh through-line device (the equivalent of Rules / Secrets / Quests) so the section feels like one system, not a list.
- Tag **≤2 CASE_STUDY** slides (band 1–2).
- Final teaching step = the bridge: value summary + proof + "what's next" — **no price, no product name, no enroll CTA**.

## Phase 4 — Purpose Pitch (slides 61–100)
- N.E.E.I.T. + 4 Quadrants. Purpose pitch: introduce the offer (exact q7 name), benefits, proof, honest scarcity, guarantee, conviction CTAs.
- **Signature close:** design a fresh manifesto-grade emotional close (Phase 4 Q4 tone: unwavering conviction) — it must land as hard as The Quest's fill-in-the-blank manifesto, in the client's own voice.

## Refrain & tone policy
- Devices are bespoke, but discipline still applies: a small set of recurring refrains, reused as callbacks; no new refrain every slide.
- Every phase carries its labeled name + purpose slide (Directive 10) before its content.
