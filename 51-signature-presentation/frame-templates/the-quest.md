# Frame — The Quest

> Derived from the Quest archetype (a purpose/destiny coach). The teaching spine is a **named
> Blueprint organized as Quests**. This is the frame Prime Directive 13 singles out — its
> **poetic, fill-in-the-blank manifesto ending** is the exemplar to study. The Quest is the
> recommended golden-regression frame. Every frame runs the identical 4-phase skeleton; The Quest
> only changes the teaching devices, refrains, and the close. The non-negotiables (slide ranges,
> ≥100 floor, ≤2 case studies, 3–7 steps, suggested image per slide, N.E.E.I.T./4-Quadrant,
> Phase-3-no-pitch, central + section hooks) are enforced by the provers, not by this template.

- **signature_frame id:** `quest`
- **Teaching unit:** a **named Blueprint** whose steps are grouped into **Quests** (3–7 steps total, grouped into a small number of Quests)
- **Per-step shape:** a step within a Quest, each Quest carrying **named affirmations** (e.g. Openness, Authenticity, Revelation, Love, Confidence)
- **Signature devices:** the RICHEST hashtag-driven narrative with a **recurring motif hashtag** threaded from avatar through close; a riddle; a definition-pair (e.g. "flair vs flare"); a well-known literary passage
- **Central hook:** the one repeated chorus line; **section hooks:** one per phase

## Phase 1 — Avatar Section (slides 1–11)
- N.E.E.I.T. + 4 Quadrants. Open in the audience's current state told in hashtags: "Is my time ever going to come?", "I'm just stuck," the exhaustion of proving your worth, an "alone" refrain, an "I wanna be successful" refrain, a "win my way" refrain.
- Establish the **recurring motif hashtag** here — it recurs every phase. Section-hook slide for Phase 1.

## Phase 2 — Signature Story (slides 12–24)
- N.E.E.I.T. + 4 Quadrants. A hashtag-told life narrative: upbringing, a schooling chapter with a "people who look like me" refrain, love/family, a hard chapter told through a "whispers" refrain, "I Deserve To Win," a pivot refrain.
- Keep the motif hashtag alive. End on a gripping "why". Section-hook slide for Phase 2.

## Phase 3 — Transformational Teaching (slides 25–60) — NO PITCHING
- The teaching is the **named Blueprint**: 3–7 steps grouped into Quests (e.g. a Purpose Quest, a Confidence Quest, a Value Quest).
- Each Quest: intro → its steps (mix questions, stories, definitions, formulas, media moments) → **named affirmations** for that Quest.
- Devices: the riddle; the definition-pair; the literary passage; a memorable protocol name; a line like "minimizing yourself is putting yourself on sale." Tag **≤2 CASE_STUDY** slides (band 1–2).
- Final teaching step = the bridge: value summary + proof + "what's next" — **no price, no product name, no enroll CTA**.

## Phase 4 — Purpose Pitch (slides 61–100)
- N.E.E.I.T. + 4 Quadrants. Purpose pitch: introduce the offer (exact q7 name), benefits, proof, honest scarcity, guarantee, conviction CTAs.
- **Signature close (Directive 13 exemplar):** a rewritten **poetic manifesto** that ends on a **fill-in-the-blank affirmation** the audience completes in their own words (e.g. "I AM BOLD UNSTOPPABLE ______"). This ending is the frame's crown — author it to Example-3 grade.

## Refrain & tone policy
- One recurring motif hashtag is the spine; layer secondary refrains around it. Callbacks over new inventions.
- Every phase carries its labeled name + purpose slide (Directive 10) before its content.
