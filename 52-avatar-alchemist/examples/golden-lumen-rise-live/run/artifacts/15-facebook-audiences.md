# Facebook Targeting Intelligence (Lumen Rise Collective)

Built on a disciplined 7-Tier Facebook Ad Targeting Framework. Every layer stays inside the curated cheat-sheet and holds each audience within the 1–10 million discipline, so the ad account never buys reach it cannot convert.

## Targeting Group 1 — The Positioning-Curious Founder

- Layer 1 (Interest): Marie Forleo, Amy Porterfield, StoryBrand / Donald Miller, Jasmine Star, personal-branding, small-business marketing.
- Layer 2 (Behavior): engaged shoppers, small-business-owner and page-admin behaviors, purchasers of online courses.
- Layer 3 (Demographic): women, 30–55, business-owner or self-employed job titles.
- Estimated size: ~2.5–4M — the warmest core; she already believes positioning matters.

## Targeting Group 2 — The Credentialed Coach / Consultant

- Layer 1 (Interest): International Coaching Federation, life-coaching, business-coaching, consulting, Kajabi, HoneyBook.
- Layer 2 (Behavior): engaged with coaching/consulting tools, small-business software users.
- Layer 3 (Demographic): women, 32–52, master's-degree audiences where available.
- Estimated size: ~1.5–3M — real practices, real results, thin visibility.

## Targeting Group 3 — The Service-Business Owner Widening Out

- Layer 1 (Interest): Female Entrepreneur Association, Boss Babe, Create & Cultivate, women in business, service-based business.
- Layer 2 (Behavior): engaged shoppers, event attendees, small-business owners.
- Layer 3 (Demographic): women, 30–55, US metro and suburban markets.
- Estimated size: ~4–8M — broader prospecting for the same overlooked-authority avatar.

## Targeting Group 4 — The Lookalike and Retargeting Layer

- Layer 1 (Interest): 1–2% lookalike of discovery-call bookers and page engagers; exclude existing clients.
- Layer 2 (Behavior): video-viewers (50%+), landing-page visitors, email-list custom audiences.
- Layer 3 (Demographic): women, 30–55, mirrored from the converting core.
- Estimated size: ~1–2M — the highest-intent retargeting pool, run last and warmest.

## Targeting Group 5 — The Author and Thought-Leader Adjacent

- Layer 1 (Interest): TED, TEDx, Seth Godin, Brene Brown, keynote-speaking, published-author and personal-brand-book audiences.
- Layer 2 (Behavior): engaged with publishing/speaking tools, long-form-content consumers.
- Layer 3 (Demographic): women, 35–55, established professionals.
- Estimated size: ~2–4M — founders who want earned authority, not just leads.

## Targeting Group 6 — The Warm-Content Nurture Pool

- Layer 1 (Interest): engaged with Amara's organic content, saved posts, story-poll responders.
- Layer 2 (Behavior): 3-second and 15-second video-viewers, profile-visitors, link-clickers.
- Layer 3 (Demographic): women, 30–55, mirrored from the engaged core.
- Estimated size: ~1–3M — already warm, run for the discovery-call CTA, not cold intro.

## How the 7-Tier Framework Is Applied

The seven-tier framework layers interest, behavior, and demographic filters so every audience stays inside the one-to-ten-million discipline: broad enough for Meta's algorithm to optimize, narrow enough that the founder never pays for reach she cannot convert. Prospecting groups (1, 2, 3, 5) run first to fill the top of the funnel; the nurture and retargeting pools (4, 6) run last and warmest, catching the founders who engaged but did not yet book. Exclusions matter as much as inclusions: existing clients and recent bookers are excluded from cold sets so budget is never spent re-selling the already-sold. Every layer traces back to the same overlooked-authority avatar, so the whole account speaks to one woman, not a demographic average — which is exactly why the cost per booked call stays low as spend scales.
