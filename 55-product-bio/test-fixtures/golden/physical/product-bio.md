9. What can I actually cook in it? Nearly everything — seared steaks and chops, crispy potatoes, cornbread and cobblers, fried eggs, roasted vegetables, deep-dish pizza, even a simmering braise that goes stovetop to oven.
10. How long will it last? Properly cared for, a lifetime and beyond — these pans are routinely handed down for generations, which is the entire point of buying iron instead of a coating.
