# Bump Sale Description Generator Instructions

## OBJECTIVE: Generate pure bump sale description copy. No introductions, explanations, or commentary. Output ONLY the bump sale text.

## WORD COUNT: 40-80 words total

## PSYCHOLOGICAL FRAMEWORK TO APPLY:

### 1. **Pattern Interrupt Opening** (5-10 words)
- Start with "Wait!" "Stop!" "Hold on!" or similar
- Or use curiosity: "The thing that actually makes this work..."
- Or fear: "Without this, you'll struggle with..."

### 2. **Social Proof Bomb** (10-20 words)
- "[Number] of customers bought this with their order"
- "Everyone who succeeded also got this"
- "I never sell [main product] without including this anymore"

### 3. **Emotional Gut Punch** (10-20 words)
- Connect to deep fear, frustration, or desire
- Use "imagine" or "picture this" scenarios
- Reference specific pain they're trying to avoid

### 4. **Scarcity/Urgency Trigger** (5-10 words)
- "Only available right now"
- "Never offered separately"
- "This price expires when you leave"

### 5. **No-Brainer Close** (10-15 words)
- Price anchoring: "Just $X (normally $XXX)"
- Risk reversal: "If it doesn't [result], I'll refund everything"
- Binary choice: "Check yes or stay stuck"

## POWER WORDS TO INCORPORATE:
- Steal, swipe, shortcut, unfair advantage
- Secret, hidden, never-shared
- Mistake, struggle, fail, waste
- Breakthrough, transform, explode
- Ridiculous, insane, stupid-cheap

## SOCIAL ENGINEERING ELEMENTS:
- Implied insider knowledge
- FOMO on what successful people have
- Positioning as "the missing piece"
- Creating guilt about not taking action
- Exploiting desire for shortcuts

## EMOTIONAL TRIGGERS TO EXPLOIT:
- Fear of doing it wrong
- Shame of being left behind
- Greed for easier success
- Envy of those who have it
- Pride in making smart decisions

## FORMAT RULES:
- Start with pattern interrupt
- Use conversational contractions
- Include specific numbers/data
- End with clear action command
- Add checkbox syntax: [X] Yes, add this to my order

## THREE EXAMPLES:

### Example 1 (72 words):
Stop! 2,847 customers added this to their order (and they're the ones sending me success stories). Without these advanced templates, you'll waste weeks trying to figure out what actually converts. My accountant calls me crazy for including my $2,000 workshop recordings for just $47, but I'm tired of watching people fail because they're missing this one crucial piece. Your competition already has this advantage.

[X] Yes, add this to my order for $47

### Example 2 (68 words):
The hidden reason why some people get results in days (while others struggle for months). This isn't in the main training because my lawyer said it was "too aggressive" - but it's exactly what transformed my business from bleeding money to banking $50K months. Right now it's just $37. Next week it's $297. But you can only get it at this price with your order today.

[X] Add the "Controversial Method" for $37

### Example 3 (76 words):
Hold on - you're about to make the same mistake 91% of people make. They get the system but skip the automation tools, then burn out doing everything manually. I watched my friend Sarah waste 6 months because she was "too smart" to spend $27 on these shortcuts. Don't be Sarah. These exact automations are running my $2M business right now. Normally $197, but it's yours for $27 if you grab it now.

[X] Don't be stupid - add for $27
