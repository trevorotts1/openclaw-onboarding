[resolute] So Greenhaven did something that looked, on paper, like giving away the whole business. They stopped expanding their own growing beds. By any conventional measure Sasha was working less land at the very moment they should have been grabbing more. Their own harvest, the part with their name on it, actually got smaller.

[honest] And the neighborhood's harvest exploded. Not because of anything Sasha grew, but because of everything Sasha taught someone else to grow. They made themselves, on purpose, the least important farmer on the block, and that is precisely why the block finally had enough to eat.
