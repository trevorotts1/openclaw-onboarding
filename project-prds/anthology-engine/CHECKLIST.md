TIER 2 RUBRIC: ten dimensions, each 8 or higher, no averaging, scored only after Tier 1 fully passes; the judge tier and persona are distinct from the writer tier and persona.

STRIKE CAP (two counters, never confused): participant rewrite budget 2 with gate re-entry (at exhaustion the gate offers Approve as-is or producer escalation, never a silent third rewrite); three internal QC attempts per deliverable, then HOLD plus a single deduped founder alert carrying the failing checks and the best draft. GATE SEPARATION: Gate B is never conflated with the Gate A build/merge rubric. Gate B's verdict is what the board contract consumes: the independent QC scorer's promotion of a card from review to done IS the Gate B pass surfacing on the board.
